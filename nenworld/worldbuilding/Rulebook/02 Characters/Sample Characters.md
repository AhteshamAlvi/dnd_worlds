# Sample Characters

[[00 Read Me First|Rulebook]] › Characters

Three builds, each shown at **creation** (Nen-less), **end of first arc** (awakened novice), and **year two** (professional) — demonstrating that the sheet grows in play exactly the way [[Nen Growth]] promises. All arithmetic is shown; use these as templates and calibration.

---

## Tessa Korrin — human, Street Kid → Transmuter duelist

*Concept:* pickpocket who saw a licensed duelist stop a riot with a raised hand, and hasn't slept right since. *Goal:* be untouchable. *Flaw:* can't resist an audience. *Ties:* kid sister (warm), fence who owns her debt (barbed).

**Creation (Tier 0).** Array + human (+1 AGI, +1 SPI, extra pick):
STR 10 · AGI 16 (+3) · DEX 14 (+2) · VIT 10 · CON 11 · INT 8 (−1) · WIS 9 (−1) · CHA 8 (−1) · WIL 13 (+1) · SPI 13 (+1)
**Body HP 30** (10×3) · **EVA 13** · **SR 2** (AGI 14+) · Reflex save prof
Skills: Stealth, Streetwise, Sleight of Hand (background) · Martial Arts (formative: "the riot") · Acrobatics, Perception (picks) · Insight (human pick)
Gear: knife (1d6), lockpicks, 200 ryn. Aura block: **blank**.
*In play she's a scout and second-story artist. A militia thug (P 12) is a real fight. That's correct.*

**End of arc 1 (~14 weeks, crisis Initiation in session 3).**
Awakening AP = 20 + 5(SPI +1) + 0(CON) = 25 → four dedicated surge-weeks (13×2 = +26/wk) = 129 → nine standard weeks (+6) ≈ **AP 190**. Techniques: Ten II, Zetsu I, Ren I, Hatsu I. **AO 9** (5%) · Control 1 (×2 cost) · Ten shroud Guard 2.
Divination (GM's sealed roll): **Transmuter.** The water tasted of copper and rain.
*Fights now: +9 Strike on a knife (P ~16) — she deletes thugs, and a professional's Ken (Guard 60, Soak 6) barely notices her. Ten rounds of aura if she's careful. She has started noticing her aura goes* sharp *when she's showing off — the Seed.*

**Year two (professional).** AP **800** · Ren II → **AO 64** · Control 2 (×1.6) · Gyō II, In I, Ken I · Hatsu II (budget 20).
**Ability — "Static Veil"** (TRA 100%): aura charged to biting static. Build: minor rider (numbing) 2 EP + In-woven 3 EP = 5 EP. Condition: only conducts through metal she's holding (Major +5 → banked). In play: knife + 40 Strike × 1.0 + rider = **P ~46**, hidden wind-up (her In I vs their Gyō); victims save Fort DC 12 or Numbed.
*She duels professionals as an equal, reads two bands up, and has learned exactly one humiliating lesson about Soak 80.*

## Brakk Ur-Denna — Orckin, Soldier → Enhancer wall

*Concept:* discharged line-breaker who swore to a dead captain he'd never let a line break behind him again. *Goal:* a company of his own. *Flaw:* keeps promises past all sense. *Ties:* the captain's daughter (warm), his old quartermaster who sells to both sides (barbed).

**Creation.** Array + Orckin (STR +2, CON +1, VIT +1; Thick Hide +1 natural Soak; Oathbound; Enhancement leaning) ([[Peoples (Races)]]):
STR 17 (+3) · AGI 10 · DEX 9 (−1) · VIT 14 (+2) · CON 15 (+2) · INT 8 (−1) · WIS 11 · CHA 8 (−1) · WIL 12 (+1) · SPI 10
**Body HP 42** · **EVA 10** · **SR 2** (Athletics from formative) · Soak 1 (hide) +2 (old heavy armor, −1 EVA) · Fortitude (VIT) save prof
Skills: Weapons (hafted), Perception, Endurance (background) · Athletics (formative: "the last stand at the ford") · Grappling, Composure (picks)
Gear: war maul (2d8), battered heavy armor, service medallion, 200 ryn.
*At creation he's the party's actual wall — Soak 3 shrugs knives (P ≤ 3 does nothing). The fodder phase is his to shine in.*

**End of arc 1 (~12 weeks, militia meditation program, competent teacher).**
Awakening AP = 20 + 0 + 10(CON +2) = 30 → surge ×2 × teacher ×1.5 on SPI 10: +30/wk × 4 = 150 → standard +7/wk × 8 ≈ **AP 206 → 200**. Ten II, Zetsu I, Ren I, Hatsu I. **AO 10** · Control 1.
Divination: **Enhancer** (the leaning landed; the glass overflowed and cracked).
*+10 Strike at 100% on a maul: P ~29. His Guard 10 (all-in defense) + Soak: front-line real. He learned Ten fastest in his cohort — Orckin discipline is built for it.*

**Year two.** AP **900** · Ren II → **AO 72** · Control 2 · Chū II (Enhancer: half cost — +4 attributes for 20/round), Ken I · Hatsu II.
**Ability — "The Held Line"** (ENH 100%): his Guard extends to ground he's sworn to hold. Build: barrier channel 3 EP + project Guard onto ally 4 EP = 7 EP. Condition: only while a living ally is behind him (Major +5). Vow: *"The line breaks through me first"* — Serious ×1.5 on Guard while anyone shelters behind it (audited: yes, it will kill him one day).
In play: Ken 50 → Guard 50 × 1.25 (channel) × 1.5 (vow) ≈ **94, Soak 9**, and he can lend half to the medic behind him. With Chū: STR 21, VIT 18 while it runs.
*He is now what he swore to be. The GM owes his vow a terrible night.*

## Neru Vall — Elf, Scholar → Conjurer investigator

*Concept:* archive-elf who outlived the case that mattered and reopened it. *Goal:* the truth of the Veyra fire. *Flaw:* treats people like documents — misfiled ones. *Ties:* a human partner aging fast (warm), the archivist who sealed the file (barbed).

**Creation.** Array + Elf (AGI +1, WIS +1, SPI +1; Inborn Attunement: awakening programs halved, Aura Sense usable pre-awakening; long-lived) ([[Peoples (Races)]]):
STR 8 (−1) · AGI 11 · DEX 11 · VIT 8 (−1) · CON 9 (−1) · INT 15 (+2) · WIS 14 (+2) · CHA 10 · WIL 12 (+1) · SPI 15 (+2)
**Body HP 24** · **EVA 10** · **SR 1** · Will save prof
Skills: Academics, Nen Theory, Investigation (background) · Medicine (formative: "the fire's survivors") · Perception, Aura Sense (picks — the elf trait makes the second legal pre-awakening)
Gear: case files, mentor's letter, plain robes, 200 ryn.
*Fragile as paper (Body 24 — a pistol is a death sentence) and already the party's eyes: passive Aura Sense 14 before anyone else can feel anything at all.*

**End of arc 1 (~10 weeks, self-guided meditation, halved by trait).**
Awakening AP = 20 + 10(SPI +2) − 5(CON −1) = 25 → surge weeks (15×2 = +30) × 4 = 145 → +7/wk standard ≈ **AP 190**. Ten I, Zetsu I, Ren I, Hatsu I. **AO 9** · Control 1.
Divination: **Conjurer** — motes of ash appeared in the water. Neru wept, and didn't say why.
*Combat contribution: staying alive (Guard 9 all-in, EVA 10, run). Investigative contribution: everything. This is a build the party protects because it aims them.*

**Year two.** AP **1k** (SPI 15 pulls hard) · Ren II → **AO 80** · Control 3 (drilled daily — archivists are patient) · Gyō II, Shū I · Hatsu II.
**Ability — "Card Catalogue"** (CON 100%): conjured index cards that record what Neru has studied. Build: detect one thing — "does this face/object match a filed card?" — 3 EP + Independence (3−1) 2 EP + scene duration (5−2) 3 EP = 8 EP. Conditions: a card must be filed by touching subject or true document for a full minute (Major +5); cards burn if Neru lies about their contents (Major toll +5).
In play: a conjured, *visible*, legally admissible archive ([[Conjuration]]: real to non-users) — courts love him, criminals want him dead.
*Growth trigger he's chasing: filing a card on someone who no longer exists (→ forensic echo form, 9 EP, waiting in budget).*

---

**What the three show together:** identical creation budgets diverge into scout/wall/eyes *before* Nen; SPI placement (13/10/15) silently sets three different growth curves; and each ability grew from the sheet's ties and flaws — the [[Hatsu Design]] Seed test passing in the wild. At year two, all three sit in the professional band together despite different AP (800/900/1k): bands are wide, parties hold together ([[Balance and Math]]).
