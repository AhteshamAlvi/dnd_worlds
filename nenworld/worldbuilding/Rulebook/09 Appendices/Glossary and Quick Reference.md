## Turn sequence

1. **Allocation** (free): split up to AO → Strike / Guard / Drive / Focus / Fuel. Committed = spent (× Control).
2. **Action · Move · Minor · (Reaction held)**
3. Attack: **Accuracy** (d20 + mod + prof vs EVA) → **Penetration** (P > Soak?) → **Damage** (dice − Soak → Guard, then Body).

## Core tables (condensed)

**DCs:** Easy 8 · Routine 10 · Moderate 13 · Hard 16 · Very hard 20 · Heroic 24 · Legendary 28 · Mythic 32.

**Magnitude dice:** P ≈ 5.5 × dice × multiplier — 2d10=11 · 4d10=22 · 6d10=33 · then ×10/×100/×1k rows. Fast mode: deal flat P.

**SR:** 1=5 m/s · 2=10 · 3=20 · 4=40 · 5=80 · 7=Mach 1 · each +1 doubles. Gaps: Δ2 ±2 · Δ3 no reactions · Δ5 auto-hit/uncatchable. En senses: −2 Δ for defense.

**Ren → AO:** none 2% · I 5% · II 8% · III 12% · IV 16% · V 20%.
**Control cost:** 1 ×2.0 · 2 ×1.6 · 3 ×1.4 · 4 ×1.25 · 5 ×1.1 · 6 ×1.0.
**Efficiency:** own 100 · adjacent 80 · far 60 · opposite 40 · Specialization binary.
**Recovery/hr:** active 1% · rest 5% · sleep 10% · Zetsu ×3 (defenseless) · distress ½.

**Ten shroud:** 1% AP (I–II) · 1.5% (III) · 2% (IV) · 2.5% (V).
**En:** radius = 2/5/10/25/50 m by rank; upkeep = radius/round.
**Kō:** all aura one point — Strike ×2 *or* local Soak = Guard÷5; naked elsewhere.
**Chū:** +2 attribute per 10 aura (Enhancers half); caps +2/+4/+6/+8/+10 by rank.
**Yū:** 10:1 surface · 25:1 internal · 50:1 regrow; full-action lock, no other Nen.

**Vows:** Light ×1.25 · Serious ×1.5 · Grave ×2 · Defining sacrifice ×3 · riders: Nen-loss ×1.5, death ×2 · product cap ×5 · narrow-parameter max ×10, two per ability.
**Optional-boost procedures:** +25% each (max +100%), each telegraphs.

**Death:** 0 Body → Dying → death saves (d20+VIT, 10+, three up/three down). Massive hit (≥2× max Body): Dying + 1 fail + Severe injury — never instant.

**Encounter check:** penetration both ways → one-hit check → RTD ratio (≤0.75 easy · ~1 dangerous · >2 don't) → endurance. Bands: +1 deadly-possible · +2 not by force.

**TP:** 2/session + up to 3 (danger/goal/discovery/vow-kept), +2 per arc. Aura grows by training weeks: dedicated +SPI · standard +½ SPI · surge ×2 first 4 weeks · events per [[Nen Growth]].
