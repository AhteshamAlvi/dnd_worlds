## The ten attributes (one job each)

STR power (Attack Power, never accuracy) · AGI move/Evasion · DEX **all accuracy + all precise aura work** · CON **Aura Pool + stamina** · VIT **Body HP + aura regen** · INT learned knowledge · WIS **Nen understanding** (Nen Theory) · PER **noticing + Initiative + aura-sense** · CHA social · SPI **Nen manifestation only**.
Saves: Reflex (AGI) · Fortitude (VIT body / CON systemic) · **Spirit (SPI)** vs Nen-on-soul (also **death-save mod**) · Composure (CHA) vs fear.

## Turn sequence

1. **Allocation** (free): split up to AO → Strike / Guard / Drive / Focus / Fuel, **and** choose where on the body it sits (even / concentrated / weighted — [[Aura Density and Concentration]]). Committed = spent (× Control).
2. **Action · Move · Minor · (Reaction held)** — Initiative = d20 + PER.
3. Attack: **Accuracy** (d20 + DEX mod + prof vs EVA) → **Penetration** (P > region Soak?) → **Damage** (dice − Soak → Guard, then Body). STR sets Power; DEX sets the hit.

## Core tables (condensed)

**DCs:** Easy 8 · Routine 10 · Moderate 13 · Hard 16 · Very hard 20 · Heroic 24 · Legendary 28 · Mythic 32.

**Magnitude dice:** P ≈ 5.5 × dice × multiplier — 2d10=11 · 4d10=22 · 6d10=33 · then ×10/×100/×1k rows. Fast mode: deal flat P.

**SR:** 1=5 m/s · 2=10 · 3=20 · 4=40 · 5=80 · 7=Mach 1 · each +1 doubles. Gaps: Δ2 ±2 · Δ3 no reactions · Δ5 auto-hit/uncatchable. En senses: −2 Δ for defense.

**Max AP = 30 × 1.6^(CON−10)** (derived, no cap; +3 CON ≈ ×4). Grow it by raising CON — Nen-tempering breaks the mundane cap 20.
**Ren → AO:** none 2% · I 5% · II 8% · III 12% · IV 16% · V 20%.
**Control cost:** 1 ×2.0 · 2 ×1.6 · 3 ×1.4 · 4 ×1.25 · 5 ×1.1 · 6 ×1.0.
**Efficiency:** own 100 · adjacent 80 · far 60 · opposite 40 · Specialization binary.
**Regen/hr (derived from VIT):** active VIT÷10 % · rest VIT÷2 % · sleep VIT % · Zetsu ×3 (defenseless) · distress ½.

**Density:** = region aura ÷ region SU. **Soak = density × 10.** Normal = AO ÷ 100.
**Gyō transfer %:** I 40 · II 55 · III 70 · IV 85 · V 100. **Ten containment %:** I 80 · II 88 · III 94 · IV 98 · V 100.
**Ten shroud:** 1% AP (I–II) · 1.5% (III) · 2% (IV) · 2.5% (V).
**En:** radius = 2/5/10/25/50 m by rank; upkeep = radius/round.
**Kō:** Ren→Gyō→Zetsu→Ten→Chū, all AO into one region (emergent, no flat ×2). Offense ≈ full AO delivered; region Soak = density × 10; every other region Soak 0.
**Chū:** bonus = round(k·log₂(1+density)), k=1 (2 Enhancer), rank cap +2/+4/+6/+8/+10.
**Yū:** 10:1 surface · 25:1 internal · 50:1 regrow; full-action lock, no other Nen.

**Vows:** Light ×1.25 · Serious ×1.5 · Grave ×2 · Defining sacrifice ×3 · riders: Nen-loss ×1.5, death ×2 · product cap ×5 · narrow-parameter max ×10, two per ability.
**Optional-boost procedures:** +25% each (max +100%), each telegraphs.

**Death:** 0 Body → Dying → death saves (**d20+SPI**, 10+, three up/three down). Massive hit (≥2× max Body): Dying + 1 fail + Severe injury — never instant.

**Encounter check:** penetration both ways → one-hit check → RTD ratio (≤0.75 easy · ~1 dangerous · >2 don't) → endurance. Bands: +1 deadly-possible · +2 not by force.

**TP:** 2/session + up to 3 (danger/goal/discovery/vow-kept), +2 per arc. **Aura grows by raising CON** (pool = 30×1.6^(CON−10), derived, no cap): CON breakthroughs via regimens + events; Nen-tempering breaks the mundane cap 20 — [[Nen Growth]].
**Manifestation (SPI):** d20 + SPI + prof vs DC — only after Knowledge (WIS) / execution (DEX) / resources (AP) / rules are all met. Awaken Hatsu 13 · bind Condition 13–16 · Contract 16 · unprecedented 20–24.
