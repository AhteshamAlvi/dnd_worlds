# Intelligence (INT)

[[00 Read Me First|Rulebook]] › Core Rules › [[Attributes]]

**Intelligence is learned knowledge — what you have studied, remember, and can reason out.** It is book-and-lab knowledge; the *understanding* of Nen itself lives in [[Wisdom|WIS]].

## What INT governs

- **Academics** — history, law, geography, the sciences.
- **Investigation** — searching, deducing, cross-referencing evidence, research projects ([[Social Exploration and Investigation]]).
- **Medicine** — treating injuries and illness, stabilizing the dying (Medicine DC 13), and setting a broken bone or diagnosing before [[Nen Techniques (Rules)|Yū]] can mend it; a botched complex Yū repair is a failed **INT (Medicine)** check.
- **Technology** — machines, vehicles, electronics, security, and the modern infrastructure of [[The World of Sorane|Sorane]].
- **Memory, logic, and hard reasoning** — raw INT checks for recall, calculation, and puzzle-solving.

## INT vs WIS (the knowledge split)

- **INT knows facts.** History, law, anatomy, how a machine works, what a text says.
- **[[Wisdom|WIS]] understands Nen.** Nen Theory — recognizing a technique, deducing an ability's [[Conditions]], reading intent — moved to WIS. INT reads the manual; WIS grasps what the aura is *doing*.
- The **Knowledge gate** of a [[Nen Manifestation]] ("do you understand what you're attempting?") is WIS (Nen Theory), not INT.

## The Intelligence scale (1–30)

| Score | Mod | What this INT looks like |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | −5 | Essentially no reasoning; below most animals. Vanishingly rare in a living human — even profound impairment rarely reaches here. |
| 2 | −4 | **Animal-level / profound incompetence** — cannot form plans or follow abstract instruction. Typical of an ordinary beast. |
| 3 | −4 | Still profoundly incompetent, but can be trained into simple habits. |
| 4 | −3 | Severe deficit; manages only simple, concrete tasks with guidance. |
| 5 | −3 | A little sharper, but still needs guidance for anything abstract. |
| 6 | −2 | Slow; struggles with abstraction, poor at study. (A clever animal — a trained dog, a raven — sits near here.) |
| 7 | −2 | A touch quicker, but still finds most study genuinely hard. |
| 8 | −1 | Below average; concrete, practical thinker who finds book-learning hard. |
| 9 | −1 | Mildly below average; gets by on practical sense. |
| 10 | +0 | Average adult — literate, numerate, practical. |
| 11 | +0 | A touch above average; picks things up a little faster than most. |
| 12 | +1 | Bright; picks up new fields quickly, remembers well. |
| 13 | +1 | Solidly bright; a step above the merely quick learner. |
| 14 | +2 | Educated professional — a specialist with real depth. |
| 15 | +2 | Seasoned specialist; synthesizes across their field with ease. |
| 16 | +3 | Scholar; broad, deep expertise. |
| 17 | +3 | Elite scholar; quick synthesis across disciplines. |
| 18 | +4 | Polymath — peak human intellect. |
| 19 | +4 | World-class polymath — master of several fields at once. |
| 20 | +5 | Genius of the age; the mind a generation remembers. |
| 21 | +5 | Just past the genius-of-the-age ceiling. Only a racial gift, a Mutation, or an augment reaches here. |
| 22 | +6 | Superhuman recall — holds a library in memory. |
| 23 | +6 | Superhuman analysis — solves problems the field considered unsolved. |
| 24 | +7 | Comprehends problems humans can't fully frame. |
| 25 | +7 | Reframes the problem itself before others finish stating it. |
| 26 | +8 | Strategy on a scale others can't hold. |
| 27 | +8 | Runs entire campaigns of thought in the time others take to plan one move. |
| 28 | +9 | Alien clarity — learns a lifetime's discipline in days. |
| 29 | +9 | Alien clarity that borders on prescience; masters the unprecedented on first contact. |
| 30 | +10 | Apex intellect — a mind like [[Meruem]]'s, mastering games and sciences in a single sitting. |

*Animals typically INT 2–6 (exceptionally clever ones 6–8). An [[Monsters|Awakened Beast]] that gains real intelligence climbs into the human range and beyond — which is exactly what lets it develop [[Hatsu]] ([[Nen and Nature]]).*
