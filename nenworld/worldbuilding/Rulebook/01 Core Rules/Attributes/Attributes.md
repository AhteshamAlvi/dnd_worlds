# Attributes

[[00 Read Me First|Rulebook]] › Core Rules

The ten attributes and the shared modifier scale. Each attribute owns **one** responsibility, and each has its own deep-dive page (linked below) that gathers every rule keyed to it and gives a stat-specific 1–30 scale. Skills, saving throws, and attribute generation live in [[Attributes and Skills]].

## The ten attributes

| Attr    | Name             | Owns                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| ------- | ---------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **STR** | [[Strength]]     | Physical power: melee **Attack Power** (never accuracy), grappling, shoving, carrying, breaking, weapon strength requirements                                                                |
| **AGI** | [[Agility]]      | Movement, dodge, **Evasion**, balance, acrobatics, base **Speed Rating**                                                                                                                     |
| **DEX** | [[Dexterity]]    | Precision: **all attack accuracy**, weapon control, fine motor work — and **all precise aura work**: shaping, manipulation, technique construction, Nen execution                            |
| **CON** | [[Constitution]] | Capacity: stamina, fatigue, sustained exertion, resistance to poison and disease, Aura pool                                                                                                  |
| **VIT** | [[Vitality]]     | Recovery: healing, injury recovery, **aura regeneration rate**                                                                                                                               |
| **INT** | [[Intelligence]] | Learned knowledge: education, memory, research, logic, hard science, solving puzzles                                                                                                         |
| **WIS** | [[Wisdom]]       | **Understanding**: Nen theory, Contracts, Hatsu, advanced techniques, rituals; recognizing implications; interpreting what's observed, understanding politics and interpersonal games of wit |
| **PER** | [[Perception]]   | **Noticing**: detecting aura and [[In]], reading opponents and aura shifts, traps, hidden foes, environmental awareness, **Initiative**                                                      |
| **CHA** | [[Charisma]]     | Social force, presence, leadership, deception, performance, **Composure** (resisting fear/provocation/pain)                                                                                  |
| **SPI** | [[Spirit]]       | Innate Nen **talent** ([[Nen Manifestation]]); also **Luck**, **Willpower**, **death saves**, and **Spirit saves** (resisting Nen-on-the-soul)                                               |

**Human racial cap is 20** for ordinary conditioning (24 for a rare [[Mutations|Mutation]]). The **21–30 band** is reached only by racial gifts, Mutations, temporary [[Nen Techniques (Rules)|Chū]] reinforcement, by **CON** which sustained Nen aura-tempering drives permanently into the superhuman range ([[Constitution]], [[Nen Growth]]), or other special circumstances (like a devil's fruit or other strange artifacts, rituals, etc). ## The load-bearing distinctions (players will argue all of them)

- **DEX vs AGI** — moving your hands vs moving your body. Aiming a strike, threading aura, building a construct is [[Dexterity|DEX]]; dodging it is [[Agility|AGI]].
- **DEX vs Control** — DEX is *precision* (does the aura go where you aimed?); [[Aura Statistics|Control]] is *economy* (how little boils off getting there).
- **CON vs VIT** — capacity vs recovery. [[Constitution|CON]] sizes the aura reservoir; [[Vitality|VIT]] paces its refill (and your body's). Independent by design.
- **WIS vs PER** — understanding vs noticing. **[[Perception|PER]] notices; [[Wisdom|WIS]] understands.** PER feels the foreign aura enter the room; WIS knows what technique it is and what it implies.
- **CHA vs PER** — projecting vs reading. Selling the lie is [[Charisma|CHA]]; catching one is [[Perception|PER]] (Insight).
- **SPI** does nothing on ordinary checks — it is the die for the *uncertain Nen outcome after all preparation is done* ([[Spirit]], [[Nen Manifestation]]).
