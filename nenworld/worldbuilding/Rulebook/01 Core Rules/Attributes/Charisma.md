# Charisma (CHA)

[[00 Read Me First|Rulebook]] › Core Rules › [[Attributes]]

**Charisma is force of self — presence projected outward, and steadiness held within.** It is how you move people, and how you refuse to be moved by fear or pressure.

## What CHA governs

- **Social skills** ([[Attributes and Skills]], [[Social Exploration and Investigation]]):
  - **Persuasion** — honest social force.
  - **Deception** — dishonest social force (telling the lie; *catching* one is [[Perception|PER]] Insight).
  - **Intimidation** — fear as a tool (raw bloodlust is [[Ren]], not a skill).
  - **Performance** — art, disguise-as-craft, working a crowd.
  - **Streetwise** — underworld navigation, contacts, rumor.
- **Composure** (the CHA skill for holding yourself together) — resisting **fear, provocation, mundane intimidation, pain, and interrogation.** Steadiness of self under pressure is force of personality, so it is CHA.
  - **Bloodlust ([[Ren]])** — a hostile Ren flare forces a **Composure (CHA)** save vs 10 + Ren rank + the attacker's CHA mod (their projected menace), with [[Ten]] adding its rank to your save ([[Nen Techniques (Rules)]]).
  - **Aura-dense zones** and other dread-inducing environments call for Composure (CHA) checks ([[Injury Recovery and Conditions]]).
- **Leadership, presence, and reputation** — commanding a room, rallying allies, the social weight behind Renown and faction standing ([[Social Exploration and Investigation]]).

## Notes

- **CHA (Composure) vs [[Spirit|SPI]] (Spirit save):** a *mundane* threat or a wave of fear is resisted with **Composure (CHA)**; a *Nen effect reaching for your mind or soul* — [[Manipulation]], a curse, forced Initiation — is resisted with a **Spirit save (SPI)** ([[Spirit]]). One steadies the self against pressure; the other resists another's Nen taking hold in you.
- **No roll dictates a PC's choices** — social results move NPCs, information, and circumstance; players keep the wheel of their own characters. Manipulation-type Nen is the deliberate, expensive exception ([[Injury Recovery and Conditions]]).

## The Charisma scale (1–30)

| Score | Mod | Presence & composure |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | −5 | Off-putting to the point of dysfunction; crumbles at the first harsh word. |
| 2–3 | −4 | Profoundly abrasive or timid; panics under any pressure. |
| 4–5 | −3 | Very poor presence; easily cowed and easily dismissed. |
| 6–7 | −2 | Awkward, unconvincing; rattles under provocation. |
| 8–9 | −1 | A bit forgettable or thin-skinned. |
| 10–11 | +0 | Average adult — holds a conversation, keeps their nerve in ordinary trouble. |
| 12–13 | +1 | Likeable / commanding; steady under stress. |
| 14–15 | +2 | Trained presence — a negotiator, officer, or performer; hard to rattle. |
| 16–17 | +3 | Exceptional charisma; wins rooms, weathers interrogation. |
| 18–19 | +4 | Peak human — a leader people follow into fire; unshakeable poise. |
| 20 | +5 | The human ceiling of presence; a voice that turns crowds and never breaks. |
| 21 | +5 | Just past the human ceiling of presence. Only a racial gift, a Mutation, or similarly special circumstances reach here. |
| 22–23 | +6 | Superhuman magnetism — bends the hostile, endures terror that routs armies. |
| 24–25 | +7 | Reshapes loyalties by presence alone. |
| 26–27 | +8 | Fearless before monsters; nothing shakes their composure. |
| 28–29 | +9 | A living legend's aura of command. |
| 30 | +10 | Apex presence — the force of self that founds dynasties and stares down calamities. |

*Some beings unsettle or compel by their nature (a [[Spirits|Spirit]]'s dread, an [[Sky People|Angel]]'s awe) at effective CHA beyond the ordinary scale — trait-granted, not the trained line.*
