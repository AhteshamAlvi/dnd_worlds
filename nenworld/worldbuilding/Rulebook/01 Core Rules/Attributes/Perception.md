# Perception (PER)

[[00 Read Me First|Rulebook]] › Core Rules › [[Attributes]]

**Perception is noticing — the senses, ordinary and aura-borne, and the speed to act on what they catch.** **PER notices; [[Wisdom|WIS]] understands.** You feel the hidden blade and the foreign aura with PER; you name what they are with WIS.

## What PER governs

- **Initiative** — reacting first is noticing first: **Initiative = d20 + PER mod** ([[Combat Core]]).
- **Passive senses** ([[Core Resolution]]):
  > **Passive Perception = 10 + PER mod + Perception prof**
  > **Passive Aura Sense = 10 + PER mod + Aura Sense prof** (awakened only)
- **Detecting aura and concealment** ([[Aura Statistics]], [[Nen Techniques (Rules)]]):
  - **Aura Sense** (PER skill) — feeling presence, direction, and rough band of unconcealed aura.
  - **Countering [[In]]** — the seeker rolls **PER + [[Gyō]] rank** vs the hider's DEX + In rank.
  - **Noticing a [[Zetsu]] "hole"** — registering the sudden absence where aura should be (a PER Aura Sense check).
- **The read game** — reading an opponent's live aura distribution is **PER (Insight) + Gyō rank** vs their DEX (aura-control) + [[Ryū]] rank ([[Combat Core]]).
- **Insight** (PER skill) — reading motive, catching a lie in the moment, reading a fight as it happens.
- **Perception** (PER skill) — ordinary senses; spotting traps, ambushes, and the hidden.
- **Chase prediction** — the route/anticipation leg of a chase is PER ([[Injury Recovery and Conditions]]).

## Notes

- **PER vs [[Charisma|CHA]]** — catching a lie is PER (Insight); telling one is CHA (Deception).
- **Combat awareness** — surprise, flat-footedness, and spotting an attacker before initiative all run on PER (vs the attacker's Stealth / [[In]]/[[Zetsu]]) ([[Combat Core]]).
- **Chū** can temporarily sharpen the senses (density → +PER for dynamic vision, which also lowers a speed-gap Δ by up to 2 for reaction purposes — [[Nen Techniques (Rules)]], [[Scale Speed and Magnitude]]).

## The Perception scale (1–30)

| Score | Mod | What this PER looks like |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | −5 | Oblivious; misses what's directly in front of them, always acts last. |
| 2–3 | −4 | Profoundly unaware; walks into ambushes, senses no aura at all. |
| 4–5 | −3 | Very inattentive; slow to react, easily surprised. |
| 6–7 | −2 | Dull senses; misses tells and hidden things. |
| 8–9 | −1 | A bit unobservant. |
| 10–11 | +0 | Average adult — notices the obvious, average reaction time. |
| 12–13 | +1 | Sharp-eyed; catches most tells and hidden movement. |
| 14–15 | +2 | Trained scout / spotter; reads a fight, senses aura well, quick initiative. |
| 16–17 | +3 | Exceptional awareness; hard to surprise, catches faint aura and lies. |
| 18–19 | +4 | Peak human — an elite sniper's spotter; senses a concealed [[En]], pre-empts most foes. |
| 20 | +5 | The human ceiling of awareness; effectively unambushable. |
| 21 | +5 | Just past the human ceiling of awareness. Only a racial gift, a Mutation, or a Gyō-augment reaches here. |
| 22–23 | +6 | Superhuman senses — feels aura across a district, reacts before an attack completes. |
| 24–25 | +7 | Tracks a hidden killer by the pressure of their gaze. |
| 26–27 | +8 | Initiative that always leads; nothing sneaks past. |
| 28–29 | +9 | Aware of everything within reach at once; nothing approaches unseen. |
| 30 | +10 | Apex perception — total awareness of the field, ambush an impossibility. |

*Bearers of ocular gifts (e.g., the [[Gojo|Six Eyes]], Scarlet-Eyed [[Kurta]]) read aura at effective PER far beyond the ordinary scale — trait-granted, and specifically about *seeing* aura structure.*
