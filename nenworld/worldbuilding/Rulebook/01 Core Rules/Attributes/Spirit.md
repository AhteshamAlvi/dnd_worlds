# Spirit (SPI)

[[00 Read Me First|Rulebook]] › Core Rules › [[Attributes]]

**Spirit is innate Nen talent — and the fortune, will, and soul-deep resilience that ride alongside it.** SPI touches **no** ordinary check and no combat number; it is the die you roll when an outcome hangs on something preparation can't guarantee. It is **not** aura pool, recovery, knowledge, control, or any ceiling ([[Aura Statistics]]).

## What SPI governs

- **Nen Manifestation** — the roll to make new Nen *hold*, once every technical requirement is already met ([[Nen Manifestation]]):
  > **Manifestation = d20 + SPI mod + relevant proficiency (usually Hatsu or Nen Theory) vs Difficulty**
  Used for: awakening a first [[Hatsu]], binding a new [[Conditions|Condition]] or Vow, forging a Nen **Contract**, stabilizing an experimental effect, manifesting the unprecedented. Only the *becoming* is a SPI roll — *using* an existing ability is DEX ([[Dexterity]]).
- **Spirit saves** — resisting Nen that reaches for your mind, soul, or aura: [[Manipulation]], curses, Nen-borne affliction, forced Initiation. Whether a fully-prepared Nen *takes hold in you* is itself an uncertain Nen outcome, so your spirit rolls against it (bonus = SPI mod + save prof + any defensive [[Nen Techniques (Rules)|Jū]]/[[Ten]] rank).
- **Willpower rolls** — resolve against the supernatural and the mind-bending where it isn't ordinary Composure: standing firm where the will itself is tested. (Steadying against *mundane* fear/pressure is Composure — [[Charisma|CHA]]; holding a technique while hurt is Endurance — [[Constitution|CON]].)
- **Luck** — the die for "uncannily fortunate with the strange": improbable breaks the GM leaves to fate. A high-SPI civilian is *lucky with the uncanny*; a high-SPI Nen user lands the breakthroughs others can't.
- **Death saves** — SPI is the **death saving throw modifier** ([[Injury Recovery and Conditions]]): d20 + SPI when Dying, three successes to stabilize, three failures to die. The spark that decides whether you cling on is spirit, not meat.
- **Opposed SPI checks** settle otherwise-undecidable Nen-vs-Nen collisions ([[Core Resolution]]).

## Growth and limits

- SPI rises only through **extraordinary cause** — 10 TP *and* an event: surviving death, a sacred baptism, a decade-vow fulfilled, certain [[Devils Fruits (Rules)|fruits]] or relics ([[Nen Growth]]). Human cap 20 (24 with a [[Mutations|Mutation]]); **creation cap 16**.
- Raising SPI buys **no** pool and no Soak — only better odds on the game's most important single roll and its saves. It is the rarest, most valuable advancement in the game.
- Two Nen users of identical CON/VIT/DEX are **mechanically identical in a fight** regardless of SPI; the high-SPI one simply *makes* what others only wish for.

## The Spirit scale (1–30)

| Score | Mod | Talent, luck & will |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | −5 | Nen fights them at every turn; nothing uncertain ever breaks their way; a feeble hold on life. |
| 2–3 | −4 | Cursed-unlucky with the strange; manifestations rarely take. |
| 4–5 | −3 | Poor Nen instinct; struggles to seat anything new. |
| 6–7 | −2 | Below-average talent; the impossible stays impossible for them. |
| 8–9 | −1 | A little unlucky, a little slow to manifest. |
| 10–11 | +0 | Average — the occasional lucky break; seats routine Nen with effort. |
| 12–13 | +1 | Naturally apt; risky Nen lands more often than not. |
| 14–15 | +2 | Talented practitioner — reliably manifests, resists control well, clutches death saves. |
| 16–17 | +3 | Gifted; seats Contracts and new Hatsu under pressure, uncommonly lucky. |
| 18–19 | +4 | Prodigy — lands manifestations others call impossible; fate seems to lean their way. |
| 20 | +5 | The human ceiling of talent; a once-in-a-generation Nen genius. |
| 21 | +5 | Just past the human ceiling of talent. Only a racial gift, a Mutation, or a blessing reaches here. |
| 22–23 | +6 | Superhuman aptitude — the strange bends toward them; manifests the unprecedented. |
| 24–25 | +7 | Reality gives ground; new laws of Nen seat on the first try. |
| 26–27 | +8 | Talent bordering on the miraculous. |
| 28–29 | +9 | The uncanny obeys. |
| 30 | +10 | Apex spirit — manifests what should be impossible as a matter of course; fortune, will, and Nen as one. |

*Beings born of aura and emotion (a [[Spirits|Spirit]], a [[Hollow]] that reached real selfhood) tend to high SPI — their very existence is a standing manifestation.*
