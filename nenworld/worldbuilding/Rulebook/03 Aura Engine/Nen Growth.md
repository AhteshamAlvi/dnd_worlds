# Nen Growth

[[00 Read Me First|Rulebook]] › Aura Engine

The rates file: how every Nen number on the sheet actually rises. The governing principle (from the design brief and [[Nen at the Table]]): **advancement means learning a specific new thing or growing a specific quantity — never "getting stronger" in the abstract.** Training Points (TP — earned per [[Progression and Training]]) buy *skill*; **TP never buys aura quantity.** Aura is grown with time, sweat, and events.

## Aura Pool growth

Weekly, based on how the character actually trained (player keeps a one-line training log):

| Week spent | AP gain |
|---|---|
| **Dedicated training** (≥4 h/day of [[Ten]]/[[Ren]] work) | +SPI score |
| **Standard practice** (~1 h/day around a job/adventure) | +½ SPI (round down) |
| **Neglect** | +0 (no loss — Ten holds what's built) |

**Multipliers (multiplicative, max ×4 total):**
- Competent teacher (Ren III+, has taught before): **×1.5**
- Master teacher (Ren V, or a named master): **×2**
- Crucible conditions (sanctioned danger: hostile terrain, live-fire drills, a war): **×2**, and each crucible week risks 1 Strain (CON check DC 13; [[Injury Recovery and Conditions]])
- **Fresh-nodes surge:** all AP gains ×2 during the first 4 weeks after awakening (this is why the novice ramp feels fast, then honest)

**Event gains (immediate, on top of weekly):**
| Event | Gain | Limit |
|---|---|---|
| Genuine life-or-death combat | counts as a dedicated week (+SPI) | 1/week |
| Near-death survived (Dying and back) | +10% of max AP (min +10) | 1/arc |
| Emotional crucible tied to the character's core | +5% max AP | 1/arc |
| Completing a sworn long-term Vow | +10% max AP | per vow |

**Soft cap = 2 × SPI³** ([[Aura Statistics]]). All gains ÷10 beyond it. This is the elite-rarity engine: an SPI 12 professional coasts to ~3.5k over years, then crawls — while the SPI 18 prodigy is still accelerating. Both are outworked by the SPI 14 veteran who *keeps a crucible schedule for a decade*, which is exactly the fiction's promise: talent sets slopes, obsession sets destinations.

## Technique ranks (the 15 techniques)

Per rank, per technique ([[Nen Techniques (Rules)]]; rank I programs for the Four Principles are in [[Awakening and the Path]]):

| Rank | TP | Base time | Gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| I | 2 | 1–3 weeks (technique's entry) | Prerequisites held |
| II | 3 | 4 weeks | — |
| III | 4 | 8 weeks | — |
| IV | 6 | 16 weeks | **Breakthrough required** — an in-play event where this technique was tested past its limit |
| V | 8 | 32 weeks | Breakthrough + a teacher at V *or* self-derived insight (GM) |

- **Teacher effects on time:** master teacher ×½, competent ×¾, self-taught ×2.
- **Observation learning:** repeatedly watching a technique used (3+ scenes) + INT (Nen Theory) DC 16 → ×¾ time, once per technique. Watching is not having: the check unlocks *faster study*, never the rank itself.
- Training weeks for techniques and AP growth overlap freely (it's all aura work); a week counts for both.

## Control ranks

Precision drills: hovering a coin on a thread of aura, splitting a leaf's leak, stilling a bowl you're flaring over.

| Control | TP | Time | Gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2 | 2 | 4 weeks | — |
| 3 | 4 | 12 weeks | — |
| 4 | 6 | 24 weeks | Teacher with Control 4+, or a Defining discipline (a year of daily unbroken drill in fiction) |
| 5 | 8 | 48 weeks | Breakthrough event |
| 6 | — | — | Not trainable for baseline humans: traits ([[Gojo|Six Eyes]]), rare [[Mutations]], or a lifetime (GM's gift, never a purchase) |

## Category work

- Efficiency percentages don't train — they're your birth spread ([[Types and Affinity (Rules)]]; drift is a story event, not a purchase).
- **Bell-curve bonus** (the lore's ideal regimen, [[Types of Nen]]): if the last 4 training weeks included work in 2+ categories, primary-category training gains ×1.25 (applies to technique time and AP both). Cross-training pays.
- Off-type learning multipliers (×1.5/×2.5/×4) apply to the *time* column of anything category-flavored, including ability forms ([[Hatsu Design]]).

## Raising SPI (and other attributes)

Attribute raises are in [[Progression and Training]] (TP + time). **SPI is special:** +1 SPI costs 10 TP *and* an extraordinary cause — surviving death, a sacred site's baptism, a decade-vow fulfilled, certain [[Devils Fruits (Rules)|fruits]] or relics. Human cap 20 (24 with a [[Mutations|Mutation]]). Raising SPI raises the soft cap retroactively — this is the single most valuable advancement in the game and should be treated with matching ceremony.

## Teachers, texts, and resources

| Resource | Effect | Where found |
|---|---|---|
| Competent teacher | ×1.5 AP, ×¾ technique time | Any licensed dojo ([[Society Law and Licenses]]) |
| Master teacher | ×2 AP, ×½ technique time | Earned in play; masters choose students |
| Classical texts (a school's manuals) | self-taught penalty removed for listed techniques | Libraries, guild vaults, ruins |
| Training grounds (pressure chambers, aura-dense sites) | crucible ×2 without the usual danger, limited sessions | Institutions, sacred places |
| A rival | +1 TP per arc (see [[Progression and Training]]) and the GM's favorite lever | Free; the world provides |

## Worked growth curves (calibration)

- **Riko** (SPI 14): awakens at AP 35 → 4 surge-weeks dedicated (+28/wk) ≈ 150 → first arc ends ~AP 200 with Ren I, Control 1. *Matches the novice example in [[Aura Statistics]].* ✓
- **Year two, standard practice + a good teacher** (+10/wk): ~AP 700 by year's end, Ren II–III, Control 2 → the professional band on schedule.
- **Elite in ~10–14 years** (mixed dedicated/standard, events): 4k–6k, brushing her SPI 14 soft cap of 5.5k. Past that: ~+1–2/week. Her story is done growing by volume — unless she raises SPI, swears decade-vows, or finds what's in the [[The World of Sorane|Hollowlands]]. That's the design intent: the endgame of growth is *choices*, not grinding.
