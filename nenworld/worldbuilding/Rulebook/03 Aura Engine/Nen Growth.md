# Nen Growth

[[00 Read Me First|Rulebook]] › Aura Engine

The rates file: how every Nen number on the sheet actually rises. The governing principle (from the design brief and [[Nen at the Table]]): **advancement means learning a specific new thing or growing a specific quantity — never "getting stronger" in the abstract.** Training Points (TP — earned per [[Progression and Training]]) buy *skill*; **TP never buys aura quantity.** Aura is grown with time, sweat, and events.

## Growing your aura capacity (raise CON)

Max aura pool is **derived from CON** ([[Aura Statistics]]: `30 × 1.6^(CON−10)`), so you grow your reserves by **raising CON** — there is no separate aura-point track and **no cap.** For a Nen user, the daily [[Ten]]/[[Ren]] discipline the lore insists on *is* the CON regimen: tempering the body and spirit that hold the aura. Because each +1 CON multiplies the pool by ~1.6, growth *feels* like the shonen breakthrough it is — a hard-won leap, not a trickle.

**Raising CON (+1):** the standard attribute regimen — 5 TP + an 8-week dedicated training block ([[Progression and Training]]) — with Nen accelerators:
- Competent teacher ×¾ time · master teacher ×½ time.
- **Crucible** (sanctioned danger: hostile terrain, live-fire, war): ×½ time, and each crucible week risks 1 Strain (CON check DC 13; [[Injury Recovery and Conditions]]).
- **Fresh-nodes surge:** the first two CON gains after awakening come at ½ time — the fast early ramp, then honest.

**Breaking the mundane cap.** Ordinary conditioning caps human CON at 20 — but **Nen aura-tempering is the one thing that pushes CON into the superhuman band (21–30)**, uniquely among attributes, because that is literally what years of holding vast aura do to a constitution. Past 20 each point costs **double time** (the air gets thin), but nothing walls it off. This is the mechanism behind "an above-average Nen user reaches levels comparable to weapons of war" ([[Progression and Training]]) — and why a Master's CON is frankly inhuman.

**Event breakthroughs (grant CON progress directly, on top of regimens):**
| Event | Grant | Limit |
|---|---|---|
| A life-or-death arc of near-constant combat | counts as a full dedicated CON block | 1/arc |
| Near-death survived (Dying and back) | completes an in-progress CON regimen, or ½ toward the next | 1/arc |
| Emotional crucible tied to the character's core | ½ toward the next CON regimen | 1/arc |
| Fulfilling a sworn long-term Vow | completes one CON regimen | per vow |

**Elite rarity is arithmetic, not a wall.** Reaching CON 22 (Master, ~8.4k pool) from a CON-10 awakening is twelve breakthroughs — most past the cap at double time — a decade of dedicated life. Nothing forbids CON 30; almost no one pays for it. (SPI sets neither pool nor pace — a prodigy's gift is landing the impossible manifestation, [[Nen Manifestation]], not carrying more aura.)

## Technique ranks (the 15 techniques)

Per rank, per technique ([[Nen Techniques (Rules)]]; rank I programs for the Four Principles are in [[Awakening and the Path]]):

| Rank | TP | Base time | Gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| I | 2 | 1–3 weeks (technique's entry) | Prerequisites held |
| II | 3 | 4 weeks | — |
| III | 4 | 8 weeks | — |
| IV | 6 | 16 weeks | **Breakthrough required** — an in-play event where this technique was tested past its limit |
| V | 8 | 32 weeks | Breakthrough + a teacher at V *or* self-derived insight (GM) |

- **Teacher effects on time:** master teacher ×½, competent ×¾, self-taught ×2.
- **Observation learning:** repeatedly watching a technique used (3+ scenes) + INT (Nen Theory) DC 16 → ×¾ time, once per technique. Watching is not having: the check unlocks *faster study*, never the rank itself.
- Training weeks for technique ranks and CON breakthroughs overlap freely (it's all aura work); a week counts for both.

## Control ranks

## Category work

- Efficiency percentages don't train — they're your birth spread ([[Types and Affinity (Rules)]]; drift is a story event, not a purchase).
- **Bell-curve bonus** (the lore's ideal regimen, [[Types of Nen]]): if the last 4 training weeks included work in 2+ categories, primary-category training gains ×1.25 (applies to technique time and AP both). Cross-training pays.
- Off-type learning multipliers (×1.5/×2.5/×4) apply to the *time* column of anything category-flavored, including ability forms ([[Hatsu Design]]).

## Raising SPI, CON, and other attributes

Attribute raises are in [[Progression and Training]] (TP + time). Two attributes carry special weight for Nen users:

- **CON** *is* your aura pool ([[Aura Statistics]]: pool = 30 × 1.6^(CON−10)). Raising CON is the only way to grow your reserves and the closest thing to "leveling up your aura"; it follows the CON-growth rules above (regimens + breakthroughs, no cap, Nen-tempering breaks the mundane 20). Recompute the pool from the formula on each gain.
- **SPI is special:** +1 SPI costs 10 TP *and* an extraordinary cause — surviving death, a sacred site's baptism, a decade-vow fulfilled, certain [[Devils Fruits (Rules)|fruits]] or relics. Human cap 20 (24 with a [[Mutations|Mutation]]). Raising SPI raises no pool and no Soak — it raises your odds on the game's most important single roll, the [[Nen Manifestation]] that turns effort into new reality. Treat it with matching ceremony; it's how a practitioner becomes able to *make* what others only wish for.

## Worked growth curves (calibration)

- **Riko** awakens at CON 10 → pool 30. Two fresh-nodes-surge CON blocks in the first arc (½ time) take her to **CON 12 (pool 77)**, then a third toward CON 13; she ends the first arc around **CON 13, pool ~123**, Ren I–II, Control 1. Her power that arc came far more from reaching Ren II than from the pool. ✓
- **Year one–two** (regimens + a good teacher, a near-death breakthrough): **CON 15–16 → pool 315–503**, Ren II–III, Control 2 → the professional band on schedule.
- **Elite in ~10–14 years** (steady regimens crossing the cap into the superhuman band, events): **CON 18–20 → pool 1.3k–3.3k.** There is no wall to brush against — only the doubling time-cost past CON 20 and the finite years of a life. A prodigy's edge over her was never more aura; it's the manifestations they seat that she can't. The endgame of growth is *choices* — which breakthroughs to chase, whether to pay the superhuman tax — not a ceiling.
