# Supernatural Races

[[00 Read Me First|Rulebook]] › Races

Beings arising from death, emotion, energy, or craft. Mostly GM-only ([[Race Rules Overview]]); the playable exceptions carry their whole cosmology as baggage, which is the point of playing one. Full NPC stat guidance: [[NPCs Creatures and Encounters]].

## The energy cosmology (GM orientation)

The vault's clean taxonomy, load-bearing for everything below ([[Nen and Nature]], [[Spirits]], [[Hollow]], [[Elementals]]):

| Being | Recipe |
|---|---|
| **Nen user** | Trained aura; requires genuine intelligence |
| **Monster/animal** | Natural energy; instinct-grade techniques at most |
| **Spirit** | Nen + natural energy, shaped by *collective emotion* (Blessed/Cursed) |
| **Hollow** | A dead person's lingering Nen + natural energy, shaped by *one desire* |
| **Elemental** | Natural energy alone, patterned by a place |

Rule of thumb at the table: **natural-energy beings are magnitude; Nen beings are technique.** A Gillian has oceanic Power and no reads, no vows, no adaptation. An Adjuchas has both — that's why the ladder's midpoint is the scary part.

## Vampires ([[Vampires]])

**Turned Vampire** *(restricted PC)* — human sheet, plus:
- +2 STR, +1 AGI, +1 VIT; **regeneration** 5 Body HP/round (not vs fire/beheading wounds); unaging.
- **Blood Demon Art:** a GM-co-designed mini-ability (10 EP equivalent, grows by feeding milestones, not TP) — *separate from Nen*; a Vampire may additionally train Nen like anyone ([[Vampires]] lore).
- **The hunger:** feed on human flesh/blood weekly or gain 1 Strain/week (max stacking) and Composure checks vs frenzy near blood. Feeding is the moral engine; the sheet won't hide it.
- **Ends:** sunlight = P 50/round unsoakable by Guard; beheading at 0 HP = death; silver: attacks vs you +2 and wounds don't regenerate. **Tethered:** if your Original dies, you die — your maker is a second character sheet the GM keeps.
- **Made Original** (in-play evolution, feeding-milestone-gated): sunlight immunity, tether broken, silver downgraded to irritant. Keeps the self. The campaign about *getting there* writes itself.

**Original Vampire** *(GM-only)* — born species; emotion-mimicry (Insight vs their Deception at disadvantage — the warmth is bait, [[Vampires]]); sunlight-immune; creates tethered Vampires. Stat as Master–Grandmaster band regardless of aura: the body alone is elite magnitude.

## Spirits ([[Spirits]])

*(GM-only as characters; central as world furniture)* Born with a Nen ability tied to their origin emotion; train and grow like users ("origin sets its nature, not its ceiling"). Energy bodies: mundane damage does nothing (penetration philosophy handles it: their Soak vs non-aura Power is effectively absolute); aura and Conjured matter harm them normally. Destroyed Spirits re-coalesce from their feeling's well over months/years unless the *well* is addressed — a mystery framework, not a stat.
- **Blessed:** guardianship abilities; communities worship them ([[The World of Sorane]] uses several).
- **Cursed:** walking disasters; the emergency that outranks politics.

**Spirit Tools** *(item rules — the playable face of Spirits)*: a Spirit sealed in an object. Wielder gains: the Spirit's ability as a granted Hatsu (fixed form, no growth, uses wielder's AO with the *Spirit's* Efficiency), and the Spirit's terms — every Tool has a **price clause** (a Vow the wielder didn't get to negotiate). Awake Tools talk. Strong-willed Tools test (**Spirit (SPI) contests** for control at dramatic moments — a clash of Nen wills). Treat every Tool as an NPC with an inventory slot.

## Hollows ([[Hollow]])

*(GM-only, all grades)* The ladder, statted in [[NPCs Creatures and Encounters]]: Hollow → Huge → Gillian (SF ×100, mindless, **no reads/vows/Nen**) → Adjuchas (intelligence arrives; *real* Nen begins; the reversion-and-frozen-evolution rules from the lore are story constraints, not table math) → Vasto Lorde (person-shaped calamity) → **Arrancar** (human-passing; full Nen mastery + everything eaten on the way; the top of the pyramid).
- Aura-bodied: invisible to the unawakened ([[Hollow]] — civilians die *unwarned*; this is why licensing exists, [[Society Law and Licenses]]).
- Innate techniques regardless of intellect: **Cero** (P = their band's heavy attack, fired), **Sonído** (burst +2 SR), **Garganta/Kūmon** (travel; breach events, [[Campaign Frameworks]]).
- An Arrancar *PC* is a hard veto by default: the progression path (consumption of sapients) fights the premise. The GM who runs one anyway knows what they're doing and owes the table a session-zero.

## Infernals ([[Infernal]])

**Lucid Infernal** *(restricted PC)* — fire given a person's leftover shape:
- Body of flame: fire immunity; unarmed strikes +P 10 burn rider; water/smothering: Numbed condition; their *touch* ignites the flammable (control is a discipline — Composure after any surge).
- +2 AGI, +1 PER; no VIT-based natural healing (fire doesn't knit — repairs via fuel/rest); Body HP normal (the flame-pattern can be disrupted).
- **The social truth:** most Infernals are mourned as dead and feared as bombs; lucid ones live in the gap ([[Infernal]]). The disease cosmology (carrier insects, infectious burns, cures) is a campaign framework hook, not PC bookkeeping — unless the PC wants the cure, which is a *great* want.
- Mindless Infernals: GM creatures — grief with a heat signature; put-downs are tragedies, write them that way.

## Constructs ([[Constructs]])

**Sapient Construct** *(restricted PC)* — made, then woke up:
- Chassis in place of biology: choose frame — *fleshcraft* (as human +2 VIT), *machine* (+2 STR, +1 Soak plating; healing = repairs: Craft/Technology, not Medicine; poison/disease immune), *puppet-shell* (+2 DEX, modular parts).
- **Nen requires the mind, not the meat** ([[Nen and Nature]], [[Constructs]]): a sapient Construct awakens and trains normally. SPI is rolled/assigned like anyone — souls aren't issued by factories, a fact theologians in [[The World of Sorane|Sorane]] fight about in print.
- **Maker's shadow:** someone built you; the sheet owes the GM the who and the why.

## Elementals ([[Elementals]])

*(GM-only)* Place-patterned natural energy. Scattered, they reform ("closer to disrupting a current"); bargaining means aligning with their nature ([[Elementals]] — find the version of your goal that flows their way; a social framework, in [[Social Exploration and Investigation]]). Nen comes easily to old ones and stays *enormous and simple*. Ancient Elementals are regional powers worshipped as gods; stat as Calamity-band terrain with opinions.

## Monsters and Awakened Beasts ([[Monsters]])

*(GM-only; companions possible)* Monsters: natural-energy magnitude, no negotiation. **Awakened Beasts:** intelligence crossed the line — negotiation, pacts, real Nen possible; the great beast-clans make standing pacts with families and settlements ([[Monsters]]) — a pact is a campaign reward worth more than any item, statted as: one summonable ally of fixed band + obligations that bind *both* ways. **Tailed Beasts:** nine country-scale facts of the world ([[The World of Sorane]] places them); SF ×1k; not encounters — weather systems with grudges.
