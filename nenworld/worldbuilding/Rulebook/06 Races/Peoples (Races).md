# Peoples (Races)

[[00 Read Me First|Rulebook]] › Races

The mortal, born-not-made peoples ([[Other]]). Availability tiers per [[Race Rules Overview]]. Every entry: body only — culture comes from backgrounds.

## Beastfolk ([[Beastfolk]])

One spectrum, three playable rungs (Winged and Shifters are restricted):

**Marked** *(open)* — +1 AGI, +1 WIS; senses: advantage on Perception (scent or hearing, pick); night eyes (dim light no penalty); pass among humans easily. SF ×1, SR 1–2 as human. Leaning: none.

**Half-beast** *(open)* — +2 among STR/AGI/DEX (max +1 each... take +1 to two), +1 VIT; natural weapons (1d6 claws/fangs — always armed); senses as Marked; visibly not human (social reality varies by region — [[The World of Sorane]]). SF ×1.

**Full Beastfolk (Mink-type)** *(open)* — +2 STR, +1 AGI, +1 VIT; natural weapons 1d8; hide +1 natural Soak; senses as Marked +1; base SR 2. Caps: one physical attribute 22. "A natural warrior before it ever trains" ([[Beastfolk]]) — and the package prices it. Leaning +5 Enhancement.

**Winged (Exceed-type)** *(restricted)* — small (Body HP = VIT × 2), +2 AGI, +2 WIS; **flight SR 3** (Defining trait; the reason for restriction — flight trivializes early-game obstacle design; opt in knowingly). Lifelong partner-bond tradition is culture, not compulsion.

**Shifters** *(restricted)* — as Marked, plus a second full form (beast: use a fitting animal block with your mental attributes) — transformation 1 action; triggers and control terms set with GM (moon, rage, injury — [[Beastfolk]]). The Kiriko-style flawless human form variant instead gains disguise mastery (Deception for form: automatic vs strangers).

## Elf ([[Elf]])

*(open)* +1 AGI, +1 WIS, +1 SPI; SPI cap 22; **Inborn Attunement:** awakening programs at ×½ time, Aura Sense usable before awakening ([[Elf]]: "inborn affinity for Nen"). Long-lived (centuries). Leaning: +5 Emission, +5 Manipulation. Social: every court wants one; every border guard remembers one.

## Dwarf *(open; lore stub — minimal kit, expand when the vault does)*

+2 CON, +1 VIT, +1 WIS; base SR 1 (never 2 by AGI); +1 natural Soak (stone-dense); advantage on saves vs poison and forced march; Craft mastery culture (background synergy). Caps: CON 22. Leaning +5 Conjuration (makers). Lifespan ~300.

## Orckin and Goblinkin ([[Orckin]])

**Orckin** *(open)* — +2 STR, +1 CON, +1 VIT; Thick Hide (+1 natural Soak); **Oathbound:** advantage on Composure to keep a stated oath, and time-tested oath-Vows read one sincerity grade higher in the [[Conditions Vows and Risk (Rules)|audit]] (their culture *builds* believers — the lore's obligation-web, priced). Caps STR 22. Leaning +10 Enhancement — "which has cost more than one opponent dearly against an Orckin Manipulator" ([[Orckin]]; the leaning is a bet, not a fact).

**Goblinkin** *(open)* — +2 DEX, +1 INT, +1 AGI; small frame (Body HP = VIT × 2.5, round down); base SR 2; **Scavenger's Eye:** advantage on Technology and Craft with salvage; tinker kit culture. Caps DEX 22.

## Merfolk ([[Merfolk]])

**Fish-tailed** *(regional)* — +1 CON, +1 WIS, +1 CHA; **aquatic:** swim SR 4, water-breathing, depth-adapted senses; **on land:** SR 0 unaided (mobility gear or carried; some learn short-term leg-shaping — GM). The trade is real; ocean campaigns only, or bring a very good wheelchair-boat.

**Fishman** *(open)* — +2 STR, +1 VIT, +1 CON; amphibious (swim SR 3, land SR 1–2 normal); water-brawler: underwater combat penalties waived, and STR checks vs anyone swimming at advantage; +1 natural Soak (scale). Caps STR 24 underwater / 22 dry. "Many times a human's power… more still underwater" ([[Merfolk]]).

**Imuchakk** *(regional — frozen north)* — SF ×2 (Body HP ×2, Soak ×2 vs the world's smallness), +2 STR, +2 VIT; cold immunity (environment), **heat vulnerability** (disadvantage on heat saves); hunt-rite culture as background. Giant-blooded Merfolk ([[Merfolk]]).

## Fairy ([[Fairy]])

*(open — and deliberately strange)* Tiny: Body HP = VIT × 1 (a *pistol* is artillery); EVA +4 (size); base **flight SR 3**, ground SR 0. **Pound-for-pound impossibility:** STR score works at full value despite the frame ([[Fairy]] lore) — a Fairy with STR 14 arm-bars a soldier, and aura Strike cares nothing for size. Grapple rules already handle leverage-aura ([[Combat Core]]). +2 AGI, +1 DEX, −2 STR cap (18). Hidden-people culture: being *believed in* is a threat ([[Fairy]]). Leaning +5 Transmutation.

## Plantfolk ([[Plantfolk]])

*(open; Treants GM-only)* +2 CON, +2 WIS, −2 AGI (and AGI cap 16); photosynthetic (needs sun/water/soil — a week deprived = Exhausted); **slow perfection:** natural healing ½ rate but heals *everything* short of fire eventually, including lost limbs (months); **fire vulnerability:** fire damage ×2 and fire injuries don't self-mend. **Long patience:** time-term Vows ("for a decade…") read one grade higher in the audit ([[Plantfolk]] lore, priced). Lifespan centuries. Leaning +10 Conjuration/Conditions-flavored design.

## Sky People ([[Sky People]])

*(regional — high places; Angels GM-only)* +1 AGI, +1 WIS; **wings:** glide (never fall-damaged while conscious; horizontal glide SR 2), true flight only in thin high air (regional) or with Nen Drive later; altitude-adapted (no thin-air penalties; lowland *rich* air is heavy for a week). Wingless native tribes: swap wings for +1 CON and mountain-goat footing (Athletics advantage on climbs).

## Variant Tribes ([[Variant Tribes]])

*(open)* Human package (Adaptable, Fast Learner) with **one dial turned** ([[Variant Tribes]]); pick the tribe:
- **Longarm:** melee reach 3 m; grapple advantage.
- **Longleg:** sprint +1 SR (movement only, not reactions).
- **Longneck:** vantage (see over cover/crowds; +2 Perception overwatch).
- **Three-eye:** +1 WIS, and the third eye reads aura — eyes-[[Gyō]] at ×½ upkeep once awakened.
Cross-fertile with humans; mixed children usually carry the dial at half strength ([[Race Rules Overview]] mixed ancestry).

## Saiyan ([[Saiyan]])

*(restricted — the progression is the problem, on purpose)* +2 STR, +1 AGI, +1 VIT; caps STR/AGI/VIT 24.
- **Zenkai:** each time the Saiyan recovers from Dying, permanently gain a **free CON breakthrough** (one completed CON regimen — which ×1.6s the aura pool, [[Nen Growth]]) and +1 to one physical attribute (max 5 lifetime activations). The lore's near-death engine — and the reason for the restriction: this PC is *rewarded* for the party's worst days, and their curve bends above everyone's. The GM must want that at the table. It also makes them a target: the trait is known, and there are people who *farm* Saiyans carefully. That is a campaign.
- **The tail:** if kept — under a full moon and open sky, Composure (CHA) DC 16 or the Oozaru takes the table (GM-controlled rampage, SF ×10). Most cut it. Keeping it is a statement.
- Leaning +10 Enhancement. Scattered, homeland-less ([[Saiyan]]).
