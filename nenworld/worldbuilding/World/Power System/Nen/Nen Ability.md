# Nen Ability

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]]

A "Nen Ability" is a special application of *[[Hatsu]]* as well as other techniques: basic, intermediate, and advanced. It is based on one of the 6 different aura types and branching off from them.

A good Nen ability reflects a person's own character and idiosyncrasies, regardless of its simplicity or complexity. Furthermore, it is best if someone creates their own ability from scratch; if an ability can be achieved through Nen, then it is possible for anyone to learn how to use it, however one can never truly master Nen if they only copy someone else's abilities. Nonetheless, it is possible for multiple individuals to develop the same ability independently, so having similar abilities is not a negative, the point is for a practitioner to make said ability on their own through their own reasoning. 

Since they can have an immensely vast range of effects, Nen abilities may be recognized as any manifestation of one's Nen that cannot be ascribed to one of the basic or advanced techniques. However, there are instances of Nen abilities being essentially applications of some basic, intermediate, or advanced technique with special conditions or restrictions.

For how a practitioner actually builds one — the seed, its self-made forms and stages, and how new stages are earned in play rather than handed out — see [[Developing a Nen Ability]].
