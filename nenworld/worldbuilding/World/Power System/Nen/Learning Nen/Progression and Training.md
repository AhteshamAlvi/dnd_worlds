# Progression and Training

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]] › [[Learning Nen]]

Orthodox Nen training begins with meditation exercises, which are aimed at allowing students to gradually become aware of their aura flow even before their aura nodes are opened and to start to control it.

What follows are the basics of aura manipulation, even if the student is still incapable of seeing aura. In this phase, the student is to place their hands in front of their chest and imagine forming a ball between them. This exercise is meant to develop the student's ability to feel their own aura. 

Students then generally learn to open the aura nodes in their eyes. Once all of the student's nodes are opened, they are introduced to the first principle, *[[Ten]]*, learning to contain the leaking aura little by little through breathing exercises. Next is *[[Ren]]*, which in turn leads to *[[Gyō|Gyo]]* and other techniques. *[[Hatsu]]* is first trained through the Water Divination exercise, by having the student attempt to produce a more pronounced change. 

Regardless of their level, it is advised that Nen practitioners train their *[[Ten]]* and *[[Ren]]* daily, since they are the foundations of all advanced techniques. Trained properly, an above average Nen user can reach levels of strength, speed and lethality comparable to modern day weapons of war. Even powerful Non-Enhancers can achieve Mach speeds and Megaton strengths with basic, intermediate, and advanced Nen control techniques alone. Enhancers can go even beyond that due to it being their specialization.
