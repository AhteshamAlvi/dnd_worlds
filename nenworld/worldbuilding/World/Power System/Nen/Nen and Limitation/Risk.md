# Risk

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]] › [[Nen and Limitation]]

Together with emotion, Risk is one of the factors which has a remarkable effect on Nen. Self-imposed stipulations that are harmful or potentially so to the Nen user, such as willingly limiting the use of their senses, can increase the power of their Nen, and of their Nen abilities in particular. These tradeoffs are once again, determined by a Nen user’s internal locus of determination.
