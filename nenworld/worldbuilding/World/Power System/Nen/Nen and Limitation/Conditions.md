# Conditions

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]] › [[Nen and Limitation]]

The creator of a Nen ability can include Conditions in it to increase its power or to make the ability feasible in the first place, whether because the user lacks the degree of skill otherwise required, or because the effects of the ability exceed what can be achieved with Nen without a trade-off. 

Conditions are based on the principle of equivalent exchange. This principle is determined by one’s own internal locus of equivalence and biases on a subconscious level. One cannot trick their own deepest sense of understanding. Thus the ideology, world outlook, and sense of right and wrong, and so on of the ability's creator plays a fundamental role in determining the exact circumstances and boundaries of a condition. 

However conditions may occasionally be rendered redundant by high proficiency in Nen, and thus are removed. It is not possible to violate the conditions of an ability without introducing some kind of consequence by making a Binding Vow.

Many Nen abilities can be activated only after fulfilling certain conditions, which are known as Activation Requirements. Most activation requirements are tasks or conditions that are simple to carry out or fulfill. Other abilities might require outside circumstances that the Nen user has no control over. Some abilities have multiple activation requirements. Certain abilities have interchangeable requirements. Activation requirements may also be continuous, meaning that as soon as they are no longer being performed, the connected ability is canceled. Some activation requirements are common to many, if not all, Nen abilities with similar effects.

Additional conditions unrelated to paying the activation cost of an ability and limiting the range of its potential usages afterward also exist. The inherent impossibility of breaking such restrictions allows the user to turn them to their advantage. Some conditions are deeply tied to the user’s emotions, to the point of being implemented simply because they "feel right". There are also conditions that do not regulate the manner in which a Nen ability is to be used, but which impact the user negatively in return for use of the ability. Unlike the penalties that some limitations carry, they are not activated when a certain rule is broken and do not impair the user’s Nen abilities, but may function as a toll of sorts. 

In general, it does not appear to be possible to break a condition (without some kind of Binding Vow); however, there are Nen users who create additional abilities to bypass certain restrictions of others, so as to be capable of circumventing them under very specific circumstances.
