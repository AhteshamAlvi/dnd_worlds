# Fū

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]] › [[Advanced Techniques]]

**Fū** ("Enclose") is an advanced form of *[[En]]* and *[[Hatsu]]*. The Nen user uses *[[En]]* to project their aura into the shape of a sphere, and then with *[[Hatsu]]*, the practitioner defines a “boundary” at the edge of their *[[En]]*. This form of projected aura with a boundary at the edge is *Fū*. 

With *Fū*, the sensory effect of *[[En]]* is focused along this boundary rather than the interior of the *[[En]]*, thus while the user loses the ability to sense things within the *[[En]]*, that sensory effect on the boundary is increased significantly, and the user is able to instantly sense anything that crosses that boundary. More importantly this boundary, while not being physical or solid, is a defined Rule of Nen. Thus other Nen that cross that boundary face resistance, and the boundary greatly opposes area of effect abilities.

One main use for defining a boundary on the border of one’s *[[En]]* is that it creates a dampening effect on the effects of other Nen abilities that cross that boundary (though it has no effect on Nen abilities used within the boundary), and hard resistance to area of effect abilities. Thus one of the main uses of *Fū* is in countering AOE abilities, although some Nen users can utilize the boundary effects with different conditions and contracts to form unique styles. Varying types of *Fū* may be created with unique methods to defend oneself or even function as an attack.
