# Advanced Techniques

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]]

After acquiring a working command of the basics and intermediary techniques, a student explores a series of advanced techniques that often combine and/or derive from the Four Major Principles, the intermediary techniques. These techniques vastly expand the user's options in combat and other uses, but they also consume more aura than the basic and intermediate techniques.
