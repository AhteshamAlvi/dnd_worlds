# Yū

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]] › [[Advanced Techniques]]

**Yū** ("Heal") is an advanced application of *Ren, Hatsu, Gyō,* and *[[Chū]]*. In effect, one focuses the effect of *[[Ren]]* and *[[Chū]]* on a particular area with *[[Gyō]]*. By doing so they focus the reinforcement on a single area like with *[[Kō]]*. However unlike with *[[Kō]]*, *[[Gyō]]* is used to hyperfocus down to the cellular level. In doing so the user can use *[[Hatsu]]* to kickstart and bolster the regenerative capability in that area, and forcefully regenerate even entire limbs, the usability, speed, and capability of which is dependent on one’s control over their aura, their skill, and their Aura quantity.

Because of the extreme focus this takes, however, it is normally impossible to maintain *Yū* while using any other application of Nen, essentially leaving one completely vulnerable. It also consumes far more Aura than any other technique. It is also far less costly to do things like reattach limbs or close surface wounds than it is to regenerate entire organs or limbs.

In addition, there are limitations to the healing ability of *Yū* largely dependent on the user and their skill and mastery, although *Yū* cannot regenerate blood because it is produced from marrow. However one can use *[[Chū]]* to speed up the production process of red blood cells and can restore their blood if they drink enough water to replace lost fluids. Things like broken bones must be set before they can be healed by *Yū* as well. Also, the brain cannot be restored through *Yū*, and its destruction still results in instant death. *Yū* is the most difficult Advanced Technique to learn and master.
