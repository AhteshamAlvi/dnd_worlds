# Intermediate Techniques

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]]

After acquiring a working command of the basics, a student is introduced to a series of intermediate techniques that often combine and/or derive from the Four Major Principles. These techniques vastly expand the user's options in combat, but they also consume more aura than the Four Major Principles.
