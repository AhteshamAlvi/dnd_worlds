# Chū

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]] › [[Intermediate Techniques]]

**Chū** (“Infuse”) is an advanced application of *[[Zetsu]]* and *[[Ren]]*. It is a primarily reinforcing technique where a Nen user maintains a state of *[[Ren]]* while closing their aura nodes with Zetsu, so instead of projecting the aura outwards, it is projected inwards into their body.

Filling their body and cells with aura allows them to reinforce their physical abilities across their entire body. This physical boost is not limited to strength, speed, and durability, but also extends to senses. This ability is most often used by Enhancer types along with different conditions. Enhancers also gain a much better multiplicative return on their use of *Chū* than others, % dependent on their mastery and specific usage of *Chū*. 

*Chū* is usually combined with techniques like *Gyō or Kō* where it is focused on a specific area (For example infusing *Chū* within their eyes can boost their dynamic visual acuity, or in their arms can boost their strength), or *[[Ryū]]* where it is distributed over the body. It can also be used with *[[Shū]]* to reinforce and energise weapons themselves rather than simply envelop them with aura. *Chū* is the most difficult Intermediate Technique to master.
