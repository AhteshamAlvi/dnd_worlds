# Transmutation

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]] › [[Types of Nen]]

If a student changes the taste of the water in the glass during their Water Divination, then that student is a **Transmuter**. 

![[transmutation.png]]

An affinity for **Transmutation** means a person can change the properties of their aura to mimic something else or create specific attributes. Transmuted aura can be made to take on the properties of natural elements, man-made substances, and even animals, among other things. Altering the shape of one's aura also falls into this Nen category.

Transmuters are not limited to mimicking the properties of solid matter. They can also give their aura different properties instead of replicating characteristics of a substance. Similar to Emission, constructs created via Transmutation are pure aura and, therefore, invisible to individuals incapable of using Nen. Transmutation is often mistaken for Conjuration due to their similarities. The difference is that Transmutation allows the user to change their aura’s properties and characteristics (often mimicking substances), whereas Conjuration changes the aura into actual substances (visible even to non Nen users).
