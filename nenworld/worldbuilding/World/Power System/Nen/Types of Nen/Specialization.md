# Specialization

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]] › [[Types of Nen]]

If a student causes any other effect than those previously stated during their Water Divination, then that student is a **Specialist**.

![[specialization.png]]

**Specialization** can be broadly defined as any abilities that do not belong to any of the other five categories, making it the vaguest Nen type. It also appears to be the rarest by a very wide margin.

In accordance with its nature, its effects are wide-ranging, therefore Specialists are not as much at a disadvantage as users of other Nen types if their category is revealed. Specialists may create abilities that don't properly belong to any other Nen type or, conversely, combine multiple or even all Nen types, including ones at opposite ends of the Nen chart, into a single ability. This extreme degree of flexibility is enabled by the unique ease with which Specialists can acquire other Nen types, which matches the affinity for their own natural category. Some Specialist abilities influence *[[Hatsu]]* itself (e.g., by appropriating others' Nen abilities or altering affinities), while others allow the user to garner knowledge that would be unattainable through other means, such as about past or future events. Other Specialist abilities have completely different effects still.

The ease with which a Specialist can acquire abilities from other types can prove detrimental: Specialists who do not become cognizant of their Nen type prior to developing an ability are at risk of misidentifying their own category, which could prove especially pernicious if Specialists are bound by the same affinity rules as other Nen users despite being an exception to the rules governing affinities.

Specialization also differs from other Nen types in that only Specialists can incorporate Specialization into their abilities. One may become a Specialist later in life, with Conjurers and Manipulators having the highest chance to do so.

It is unknown how Specialists develop their Nen abilities or how they are able to train their natural category apart from practicing Water Divination. Generally both the training methods and the development methods are unique to the user themselves. A Specialist's manifestation of *[[Hatsu]]* during Water Divination gives some insight into the general direction in which they might or should develop their ability, since *[[Hatsu]]* is profoundly linked to one’s Nen type. However, the information received this way is vague enough that it is impossible to pin down just what kind of ability a Specialist has already developed through their results during the Water Divination test.
