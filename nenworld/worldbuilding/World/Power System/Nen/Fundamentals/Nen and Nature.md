# Nen and Nature

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[Nen]]

Wild animals are more perceptive to Nen, and learn *[[Zetsu]]* spontaneously. Especially intelligent animals may learn to open their aura nodes and learn the four basic principles, and even develop Nen abilities. Animals with powerful Nen gain intelligence too (not usually on the level of humans, but especially powerful animals may achieve speech and humanlike intelligence, although this is very rare). Many such animals initiate their offspring as well. Animals born with strange mutations are often also born with the ability to use Nen (i.e. a white tiger, etc.)

This same rule holds for every non-human being capable of using Nen at all, not just animals: **Nen only comes from genuine intelligence.** A creature without real intellect runs purely on natural energy instead, and at most can pick up the [[Four Major Principles]] by pure instinct — the same way a wild animal learns Zetsu — with a rare few managing a basic [[Intermediate Techniques|intermediate technique]] the same instinctive way. What instinct can never produce is a [[Hatsu]]: a Hatsu is a deliberate, personal creation, and creating one takes the intellect to do the creating. A creature only develops real Nen abilities of its own once it actually attains that intelligence. See [[Monsters]] and [[Hollow]] for how this plays out at the extreme end of that scale.
