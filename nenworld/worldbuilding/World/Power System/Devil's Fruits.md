# Devil's Fruits

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Power System]]

Devil's Fruits are strange fruits that can be found anywhere in the world, growing at random with no known pattern to their appearance. There is no central sea, no single archipelago, and no organization that catalogues them. A Devil's Fruit is just as likely to turn up in a back-alley market stall or a forgotten orchard.

Eating one grants exactly one power in exchange for exactly one curse.

## The Power

A Devil's Fruit's power can be virtually anything, so long as it is **not Nen-based**. It does however sometimes draw on Aura as the source of energy. Turning one's body to rubber, becoming intangible, sprouting wings and flying, phasing through walls, turning invisible, etc.

## The Curse

Every fruit's power comes bound to a curse, and the curse is exactly as unpredictable as the power. There is no universal curse that applies to every fruit eaten. Some curses are mild inconveniences while others are crippling. The curse is discovered the same way the power is, by eating the fruit and finding out.

## Active vs. Passive (GM Use Only)

In-universe, as far as anyone in the world is concerned, a fruit is just a Devil's Fruit, and two fruits can look and behave nothing alike. The distinction below exists purely for the GM's bookkeeping and should never surface as in-world terminology, lore, or character knowledge.

- **Active powers** draw on the user's own Aura pool to function — things like telekinesis or flight. The Aura cost is usually quite low; the Aura spent is more of a trigger or maintenance cost than a true expenditure.
- **Passive powers** are a permanent alteration to the user's body — a rubber body, skin that cannot be cut — and require no Aura at all to use or maintain. They simply are the way the user's body now works.

## Rarity and Reputation

Devil's Fruits are rare, and the risk of eating an unidentified one is severe enough that most people who encounter one leave it alone. There's no way to know what a fruit does, or what it costs, until it's already been eaten and the change has taken hold. Because of this, eating a Devil's Fruit is widely regarded as a foolish, reckless gamble. The kind of thing done out of desperation, ambition, or ignorance, not sound judgment. Those who do it anyway, and survive the curse, are a very small and strange minority.

## Random Powers

1. Turns one's body rubber-like, stretching and rebounding like elastic.
2. Grants the ability to turn invisible at will.
3. Grants the ability to fly unaided.
4. Grants the ability to phase through solid matter.
5. Makes the skin impossible to cut or pierce.
6. Allows the lungs to draw oxygen from water as easily as air.
7. Grants telekinetic control over small objects at a distance.
8. Grants the ability to reshape one's own body like clay.
9. Grants the ability to turn any surface touched to glass.
10. Grows a second set of arms.
11. Grants the ability to see through solid objects.
12. Grants the ability to store and reopen wounds as portals to a personal pocket space.
13. Grants the ability to communicate with and command insects.
14. Turns the body into living sand that reforms after being struck.
15. Grants the ability to freeze any liquid touched.
16. Grants perfect recall of anything ever seen or read.
17. Grants the ability to split into two independent copies of oneself for a short time.
18. Grants control over one's own density, becoming as heavy as stone or as light as paper.
19. Grants the ability to grow to twice normal height.
20. Grants venom glands that can be triggered at will.
21. Grants the ability to erase sound in a small radius.
22. Allows the body to climb any surface as easily as an insect.
23. Heals any wound within seconds, no matter the severity.
24. Grants the ability to swap places instantly with any object one has touched.
25. Grants the ability to generate and control fire from bare skin.

## Random Curses

1. The eater can never learn to swim, and sinks like a stone in water.
2. The eater casts no reflection and cannot be photographed or recorded.
3. The eater cannot taste anything sweet.
4. The eater ages twice as fast as normal.
5. The eater cannot lie; every attempt comes out as the truth.
6. The eater feels intense pain whenever within sight of the ocean.
7. The eater cannot touch another person's bare skin without excruciating pain to both.
8. The eater must eat twice as much food to stay alive.
9. The eater cannot sleep more than one hour at a time.
10. The eater's shadow acts independently and occasionally betrays their location.
11. The eater cannot enter running water of any kind.
12. The eater loses all sense of smell.
13. The eater bleeds a color other than red, staining anything it touches permanently.
14. The eater cannot be within a mile of another Devil's Fruit eater without severe nausea.
15. The eater's voice is inaudible to anyone they haven't personally touched.
16. The eater cannot consume any alcohol or its equivalent without lethal effect.
17. The eater is compelled to tell the truth about their own weaknesses when directly asked.
18. The eater cannot cross a threshold (doorway) uninvited.
19. The eater slowly forgets the faces of people they once loved.
20. The eater cannot be depicted in art or writing; any attempt to do so fails or is destroyed.
21. The eater is unable to eat meat of any kind without becoming violently ill.
22. The eater's body temperature runs so cold that frost forms wherever they stand for long.
23. The eater cannot lift anything heavier than a book without their limbs seizing up.
24. The eater loses a memory of their choosing every time they use their power.
25. The eater is allergic to their own power's element or material (e.g., cannot touch rubber, fire, ice).
