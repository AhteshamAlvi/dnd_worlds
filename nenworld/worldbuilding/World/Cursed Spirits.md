# Cursed Spirit

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Cursed Spirits are born from human negativity — fear, shame, grudges, humiliation — condensed until it takes on a will and a body of its own. A spirit's shape, power, and personality all trace back to whatever emotion or event spawned it: one born from the collective fear of a specific animal might look and act like that animal given monstrous form; one born from a single person's grudge might be built entirely around finding and destroying whoever wronged them.

Because they're made of concentrated negative feeling rather than flesh, Cursed Spirits are naturally invisible and intangible to most people — only those with the right sensitivity can perceive or fight them directly, which is exactly why they've historically been able to live and hunt among the human population largely undetected. A spirit that's destroyed doesn't necessarily stay gone forever if the emotion that birthed it is common and strong enough; new, similar spirits can and do reform from the same well of feeling.

Cursed Spirits range enormously in intelligence and temperament — some are barely more than animals acting on instinct, others are as cunning and self-aware as any human, and a handful are old and powerful enough to have built their own goals, alliances, and rivalries independent of whatever emotion first made them.
