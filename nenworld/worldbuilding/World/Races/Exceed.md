# Exceed

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Exceed are a small, cat-like people — upright, talking, fully sapient, usually no bigger than a housecat — with the inborn ability to sprout wings and fly for short stretches. Most are hatched from eggs rather than born live, and it's an open, slightly uncomfortable mystery among Exceed themselves exactly what determines which family an egg turns up in.

What makes Exceed unusual isn't just the size or the flight — it's the bond. Exceed have a strong cultural pull toward partnering with a single human or other person for life, less pet-and-owner than genuine friendship between equals, and an Exceed separated from their partner for too long tends to suffer for it. Personality-wise they run the full range — blunt, sweet, vain, anxious, arrogant — same as any person; the cat features are physical, not behavioral.

Exceed have no cities of their own to speak of. They live embedded in human and other populations, one per household or partnership, which makes them one of the few races on this list with essentially no separate homeland or society at all.
