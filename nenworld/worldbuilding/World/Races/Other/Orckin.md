# Orckin

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Orckin are the peoples the word "monstrous" gets thrown at by everyone who has never actually dealt with them. They're heavyset and powerfully built, with tusked jaws, thick hide, and colouring that runs grey, green, or slate — physically imposing enough that a great deal of the world assumes them dangerous on sight. The assumption is wrong in the way that matters: Orckin are a *people*, with languages, histories, crafts, laws, and homelands, and the line between them and [[Monsters]] isn't ferocity but culture. A Monster has no common ground to offer. An Orckin has terms.

They come in more than one build. The larger kind — what most people mean by the name — stand a head or more above a human and are strong enough that Orckin labour and Orckin mercenaries are prized wherever anyone is honest enough to hire them. The smaller kind, usually called **Goblinkin**, are wiry and short, quick-handed and quick-thinking, and have a long tradition of tinkering, scavenging, and building clever things out of what larger peoples throw away. The two share ancestry, frequently share settlements, and consider the distinction roughly as significant as humans consider height.

What Orckin culture is actually built around is obligation. Most Orckin societies run on explicit debts and stated oaths rather than inherited rank — who owes what to whom, said out loud, in front of witnesses — which outsiders often mistake for bluntness or aggression when it's closer to the opposite. An Orckin who has given their word on something is generally more reliable than the contract a human would have written instead, and a great deal of Orckin resentment toward other peoples traces back to bargains that were struck properly and then broken casually.

Orckin learn [[Nen]] like anyone else, and their physical build gives them an obvious head start in [[Types of Nen|Enhancement]] — a leaning strong enough that outsiders assume it's the only thing they're good for, which has cost more than one opponent dearly against an Orckin Manipulator.
