# Fairy

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Fairies are a tiny, hidden people, humanoid but only inches tall, usually winged, living in concealed communities tucked into forests, gardens, and the overlooked corners of the larger world. Their size makes them easy to dismiss as harmless or mythical, which is exactly how they prefer it: most fairy communities survive precisely because the big folk don't believe they're real, and they guard that secrecy carefully.

That small stature hides a wildly disproportionate strength. A Fairy can be pound-for-pound many times stronger than a human, fast enough to be hard to even see, and a serious threat to something a hundred times its size. The best-known are a warren-dwelling people who live underground in vast secret colonies. Like any race here, Fairies can also learn [[Nen]], which on a body that small can be devastatingly effective.
