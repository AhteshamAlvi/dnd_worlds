# Variant Tribes

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Variant Tribes are peoples who are almost entirely human but for a single exaggerated trait carried down their whole lineage — arms long enough to reach the ground, legs built for enormous strides, necks that extend well past a human's, an extra eye set in the brow. Each tribe breeds true for its one distinguishing feature and is otherwise indistinguishable from ordinary people: they live in ordinary settlements, hold ordinary jobs, and marry within their own tribe to keep the trait intact.

The traits are rarely just cosmetic. Long arms make for reach and leverage a normal fighter can't match; long legs, blinding ground speed; a third eye, a wider or stranger field of perception. None of it makes a Variant a different species — they're human, cross-fertile with baseline humans, and as capable of [[Nen]] as anyone. They're simply humanity with one dial turned far past the usual setting.
