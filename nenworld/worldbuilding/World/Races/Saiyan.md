# Saiyan

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Saiyans look human enough to pass at a glance — until the tail. Born with one, and with a physiology that gets stronger the closer it comes to dying, a Saiyan who survives a near-fatal fight comes back from it tougher than before, which makes them a people who treat combat less as a last resort and more as a growth opportunity. Left unchecked, that same instinct produces a species with a real fondness for violence and a competitive streak that doesn't fully switch off even among friends.

The tail isn't just decoration. Under the right conditions — traditionally, looking up at a full moon — it lets a Saiyan transform into a towering, mindless ape form of immense destructive power, which is exactly as dangerous to allies as it is to enemies and is the reason plenty of Saiyans choose to have the tail removed rather than risk losing themselves to it. Beyond that, with enough raw strength, rage, or willpower, some Saiyans can unlock still further transformations — heightened states that dramatically amplify their power at a real cost to control and stamina.

Saiyans have no single homeland left in this world; they live scattered and mostly assimilated, recognizable by temperament and, for those who kept it, the tail.
