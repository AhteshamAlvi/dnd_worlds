# Fanalis

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Fanalis are a warrior people, human in shape but built entirely differently underneath — red hair, sharp canines, and a body that's naturally faster, stronger, and tougher than an ordinary human's by a wide margin, with no magic or special training required to get there. A Fanalis child can out-fight grown adults of other races well before reaching maturity, and a trained adult Fanalis is a match for small squads on their own.

That raw physical gift has made Fanalis a historically exploited people — prized and hunted for their strength, plenty of their history involves being captured, sold, or pressed into service as soldiers, gladiators, or bodyguards rather than left to govern themselves. What's left of Fanalis society tends to be small, scattered, and wary of outsiders as a direct result, though individual Fanalis are often found thriving as mercenaries, guards, or fighters precisely because their body makes that path so easy to walk.

Fanalis have no magical affinity to speak of beyond their bodies — everything they bring to a fight is physical, which makes them a rare "no strings attached" powerhouse race in a world otherwise thick with magic, Nen, and cursed power.
