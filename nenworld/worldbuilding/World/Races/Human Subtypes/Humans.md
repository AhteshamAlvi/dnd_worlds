## Humans


## Human Subtypes

Human subtypes are still human — same species, capable of everything an ordinary human is, and like anyone else can be a [[Nen]] user — but they carry a distinct lineage or bloodline that leaves them **physically different from the baseline** in some inherited, bodily way. They are usually either a descendent of a [[Bloodkin]], or the descendants of a hybrid between a human and a member of another race. They can also be descendants of [[Constructs]] (artificially constructed or modified beings)
