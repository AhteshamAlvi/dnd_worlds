# Fishman

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Fishmen are an aquatic people — humanoid but unmistakably not human, built with gills, fins, and features drawn from whatever sea creature their particular lineage favors, from sharks to eels to rays. They breathe water as naturally as air, swim at speeds no human diver could match, and are, on average, considerably stronger than humans of the same size thanks to bodies built for pushing against ocean currents.

Life split between land and sea has left Fishmen with their own distinct settlements, usually coastal or fully submerged, and a long, difficult history with human society — Fishmen have historically faced serious prejudice and discrimination from land-dwelling humans who see them as lesser or monstrous purely for their appearance, which has left plenty of Fishman communities insular and distrustful of outsiders, and a smaller number of Fishmen determined to prove that distrust wrong by living openly among humans anyway.

Because their strength and speed are simply a fact of their body, a Fishman does not need Aura, magic, or a Devil's Fruit to be dangerous in a fight — those are simply additions to a baseline that already outperforms an unaugmented human.
