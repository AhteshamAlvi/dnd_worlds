# Sky People

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Sky People are the peoples native to the upper air — the high floating islands, cloud seas, and mountaintops far above where ordinary humans live. Generations in the thin, bright air have set them apart from surface humans: most are marked by small wings, feathered or otherwise, and by builds adapted to altitude and long gliding flight. Their cultures developed largely in isolation from the ground below, with their own faiths, technologies, and rivalries, and many Sky People go their whole lives without ever descending to the surface.

They aren't a single people so much as several related ones sharing the sky — winged islanders, cloud-dwellers, and the wingless native tribes who lived on the high lands before the others arrived, and who often clashed with them over territory. Physically they're close enough to surface humans to be mistaken for them at a glance, wings aside, and like any human they can learn [[Nen]].

The most rarefied of the Sky People are the **Angels**: a pale, long-lived, winged lineage that keeps to the very highest floating islands, far above even the other sky-dwellers. Angels are set apart by their beauty, their aloofness, and a raw power that dwarfs the tribes below them — enough that surface faiths and sky faiths alike have mistaken them for literal divinities, and enough that they've rarely had reason to correct the misunderstanding. They descend from their heights seldom and for reasons of their own, and an Angel taking a personal interest in events on the ground is treated as a rare and weighty thing.
