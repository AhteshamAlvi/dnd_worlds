# Demon

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

This Demon is a human transformed — once infected with a particular ancient bloodline, the body stops aging, wounds close almost as fast as they're taken, and physical strength climbs far past anything a normal person could reach. The price is steep and permanent: sunlight is lethal, hunger becomes a constant, gnawing need that can only be answered by consuming other people, and every one of these Demons ultimately answers to the will of whoever's blood turned them, whether they want to or not.

Most Demons keep some sliver of the person they used to be — memories, mannerisms, occasionally even restraint — but that sliver erodes the longer they live this way and the more they give in to the hunger. A newly turned Demon might still recognizably be someone's parent or friend; an old one rarely is.

Because sunlight is an absolute weakness, Demons live entirely nocturnal lives, and human settlements that know what to look for organize around that fact — after-dark curfews, sunlit safehouses, and hunters trained specifically to track and kill them before they can build a following.
