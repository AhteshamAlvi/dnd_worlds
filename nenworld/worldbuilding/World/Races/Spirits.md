# Spirits

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Spirits are condensed human emotion given a will and a body of its own — Nen fused with natural energy and shaped by whatever feeling birthed it. Positive emotion produces a **Blessed Spirit**; negative emotion produces a **Cursed Spirit**. Every Spirit that exists is a serious presence, because the negative and positive energies of people naturally coagulate into larger forms, like gravity pulls together stars.

A Spirit is born with a Nen ability tied directly to the its origin. For example, a **Cursed Spirit** born from the collective fear of animals looks and acts like a monstrous animal and might have some kind of shapeshifting ability. From that starting point, a Spirit can train and grow like any other Nen user, meaning its origin sets its nature, not its ceiling.

Because they're made of concentrated feeling rather than flesh, Spirits are generally difficult to kill and quickly regenerate like energy-based beings. A Spirit that's destroyed doesn't necessarily stay gone forever if the emotion that birthed it is common and strong enough, a new and similar Spirit can and does reform from the same well of feeling.

## Blessed Spirits

Born from joy, love, gratitude, hope, courage, etc. Positive emotions. Cursed Spirits range enormously in intelligence and temperament: some are barely more than animals acting on instinct, others are as self-aware as any human, and a handful are old and powerful enough to have built their own goals, alliances, and rivalries independent of whatever emotion first made them. Blessed Spirits tend toward peaceful, protective temperaments, and their abilities lean toward creation and guardianship rather than destruction, though a Blessed Spirit is still immensely powerful. Communities that know what they're dealing with often treat a resident Blessed Spirit as a guardian to be honored or worship them as a local deity, rather than treating them as a threat.

## Cursed Spirits

Born from fear, shame, grudges, humiliation, etc. Negative emotions. Cursed Spirits range enormously in intelligence and temperament: some are barely more than animals acting on instinct, others are as cunning and self-aware as any human, and a handful are old and powerful enough to have built their own goals, alliances, and rivalries independent of whatever emotion first made them. Cursed Spirits tend toward violent, destructive temperaments, and their abilities lean toward destruction and battle, and are immensely powerful. They are often regarded as walking disasters.

## Spirit Tools

A Spirit can be sealed into an object — a blade, a ring, a vessel of some kind — rather than left to roam in a body of its own. The result is a **Spirit Tool**: an artifact that carries a Spirit's power and Nen ability inside it, which a wielder can draw on as if it were their own. Depending on the binding, the sealed Spirit may be dormant and purely a battery for its ability, or fully awake — able to speak with its wielder, lend its strength deliberately, and in some cases manifest partway out of the object to fight alongside them.

Sealing is how a Spirit's power gets passed down or put to use without a loose Spirit running free: a Blessed Spirit bound into an heirloom weapon can serve a family for generations, and even a Cursed Spirit, safely sealed, becomes a source of borrowed power rather than a walking disaster. The relationship between a Spirit Tool and its wielder ranges from master-and-servant to genuine partnership, and a strong-willed sealed Spirit is not always a willing one.
