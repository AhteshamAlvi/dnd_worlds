# Monsters

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Monsters are the natural predators of the world that were never human and never civilized. A vast, varied category of inhuman creatures that live to hunt, spread, and grow. Some are little more than dangerous animals; others are cunning enough to mimic human speech and behavior as a lure, feeling nothing underneath it, using a convincing cry for help in the same breath as the kill. What they share is that there is no common ground to be found with them.

Monsters range from vermin a village can handle to individuals powerful enough to level regions and outlive empires. The strongest are less "creatures" than forces of nature, and a single ancient Monster can define the history of everything living near it.

The **Tailed Beasts** are the definitive example of that upper extreme. They are the collective name for nine titanic Monsters, each an ocean of power in a single body that were, according to the oldest accounts, once a single primordial Monster that split apart into nine. Each of the nine is a country level threat in its own right, and their scattering across the world is treated less as a fact of natural history than as a recurring catastrophe.

Like animals, Monsters with strong enough power can spontaneously develop [[Nen]], and the most powerful can reach humanlike intelligence and speech (see [[Nen and Nature]]).
