# Elf

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Elves are a near-human people, distinguished mostly by pointed ears, an inborn affinity for magic, and a long, still-unhealed grudge. Generations ago, an entire elf population was massacred by humans who wanted their magic for themselves. Rather than dying out, that grudge lingered as restless spirits, which centuries later found their way into newborn human bodies across the world — meaning a significant number of people walking around today are, unknowingly, reincarnated elves waiting to remember who they were.

An elf who has reawakened their original memories and power is a different creature entirely from one who hasn't: dormant, they're indistinguishable from a talented human; awakened, they carry both the magical potency and the inherited resentment of a people who were wiped out once already. That resentment doesn't have to define them — plenty of awakened elves choose to let it go — but it's always there to be drawn on, for good or ill.

Elves have no real homeland left. What remains of their culture survives in fragments: old techniques, half-remembered names, and the occasional hidden enclave of elves who never reincarnated at all and have been waiting, unaging, for their people to return.
