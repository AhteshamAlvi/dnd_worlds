# Kurta

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]] › [[Humans]] › [[Clans]]

The Kurta are born with eyes that change. Under strong emotion — rage, grief, resolve — a Kurta's eyes flood a brilliant scarlet, and in that state they are widely considered among the most beautiful things a person can look at. The shift isn't a technique or a choice; it's an involuntary reflex of the body, and a Kurta child does it long before they understand what it means.

That beauty is the whole tragedy of the clan. Scarlet Eyes hold their colour after death, and they are one of the most valuable things in the world to the sort of collector who deals in bodies. The Kurta learned early to hide what they were, keeping to remote settlements and concealing their eyes among outsiders — and it wasn't enough. The clan was hunted to near-extinction for the contents of their skulls, and the survivors are scattered, few, and rarely willing to admit to the name.

Beyond the eyes themselves, Kurta are ordinary humans and train [[Nen]] the same way anyone else does. The eyes do sharpen perception considerably when active — a Kurta in the scarlet state sees aura with unusual clarity — which is why the few remaining members of the clan tend to be extraordinarily dangerous when something finally makes them angry enough to stop hiding.
