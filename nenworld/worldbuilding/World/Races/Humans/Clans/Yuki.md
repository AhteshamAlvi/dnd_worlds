# Yuki

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]] › [[Humans]] › [[Clans]]

The Yuki produce ice from their own bodies. Not conjured, not shaped from surrounding water — a Yuki's physiology converts moisture and aura directly into ice at will, letting them grow it from their skin, exhale it, or raise it out of the ground they're touching. Children of the clan frost over their own hands and breath long before anyone teaches them anything, which is usually how a Yuki family discovers a newborn carries the trait at all.

Because it's physiological rather than trained, the clan runs cold in the literal sense: Yuki bodies sit at a lower temperature than a human's, they're largely indifferent to winter, and they suffer badly in real heat — a Yuki in a desert is closer to ill than uncomfortable. That vulnerability is well enough known to have been used against them repeatedly.

The Yuki's history is mostly a history of hiding. An inherited trait that visibly marks a bloodline makes an easy target during any purge, and enough rulers have decided that bloodline-carrying families were a threat worth eliminating that the clan learned to disperse, marry outward, and deny the name. Most surviving Yuki don't know they carry it until it surfaces on its own.
