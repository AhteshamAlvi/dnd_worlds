# Vinsmoke

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]] › [[Humans]] › [[Clans]]

The Vinsmoke are what happens when [[Constructs|Construct]] work is done on a bloodline instead of an individual. Generations back, the family had its own children deliberately engineered before birth — bones and skin reinforced into a flexible internal armour, strength and speed pushed well past human tolerance, healing accelerated to close serious wounds in hours. The modifications bred true, and their descendants are now born with all of it, which is what makes the Vinsmoke a clan rather than a workshop's output.

They sit in an awkward spot in the taxonomy for exactly that reason. A Construct is deliberate and reproducible; a Clan is an inherited, contained family line. The Vinsmoke are both, and the older families find them distasteful on precisely those grounds — a bloodline that was *manufactured* rather than earned through [[Bloodkin]] transformation or descended from something that occurred naturally.

The engineering came with an intended cost. The original process was designed to suppress emotional response, producing soldiers who felt little and hesitated less, and the family has spent generations treating that suppression as the point rather than the price. It doesn't always take — Vinsmoke born with their emotional range intact are treated as defective by their own house, and tend to leave it as early as they can manage.
