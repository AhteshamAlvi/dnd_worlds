# Jūgo Clan

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]] › [[Humans]] › [[Clans]]

The Jūgo Clan absorb natural energy whether they want to or not. Where any other practitioner has to deliberately reach for the natural energy around them, a member of this clan takes it in constantly and passively, the way lungs take in air — and because a human body was never built to hold it, the excess forces physical change. Skin hardens and discolours, limbs thicken or extend into shapes that aren't human, and the body reconfigures itself into something built for violence.

The transformation drags the mind with it. Absorbed natural energy brings on escalating, uncontrollable rage — the clan's members swing between long stretches of calm and sudden murderous episodes they don't choose, don't enjoy, and often can't remember clearly afterward. Most Jūgo describe it as something happening *to* them. It's why the clan has historically lived in isolation, at distance from settlements, with members frequently choosing confinement over the risk of being near people when it takes them.

The clan's blood is also the source of a great deal of misery outside it. Their tissue carries the same absorption property, so an outsider implanted with Jūgo flesh gains a crude version of the same transformation and the same rage — a fact that has made the clan a target for anyone willing to harvest people for it. The clan takes its name from its most infamous member rather than any name of its own; they never had much use for one.
