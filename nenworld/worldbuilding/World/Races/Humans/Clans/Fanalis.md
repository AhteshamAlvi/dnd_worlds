# Fanalis

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]] › [[Humans]] › [[Clans]]

The Fanalis are [[Bloodkin]] — descendants of an ancestor remade with the blood of the **Red Lion**, a [[Monsters|Monster]] species long since hunted to extinction everywhere except in the bodies of the people it made. The modification bred true, generation after generation, until what started as one desperate transformation became an entire clan.

It shows in the body immediately: red hair, sharp canines, and a physique built faster, stronger, and tougher than an ordinary human's by a wide margin — no [[Nen]], magic, or training required to get there. A Fanalis child can out-fight grown adults of other peoples well before reaching maturity, and a trained adult Fanalis is a match for small squads on their own.

That raw physical gift has made the Fanalis a historically exploited people — prized and hunted for their strength, with plenty of their history involving capture, sale, or conscription as soldiers, gladiators, or bodyguards rather than being left to govern themselves. What's left of Fanalis society tends to be small, scattered, and wary of outsiders as a direct result, though individual Fanalis are often found thriving as mercenaries, guards, or fighters precisely because their body makes that path so easy to walk.

Fanalis have no innate affinity for [[Nen]] beyond their bodies — everything they bring to a fight is physical, which makes them a rare "no strings attached" powerhouse clan in a world otherwise thick with Nen and inherited power. Like any human, they can still train Nen on top of what their blood already gives them.
