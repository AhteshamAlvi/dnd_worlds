# Gojo

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]] › [[Humans]] › [[Clans]]

The Gojo line carries the **Six Eyes** — a physical ocular trait that appears in the clan perhaps once a century, and never predictably. A bearer's eyes are structurally different from a human's, and what they do is see [[Nen]] the way no trained [[Gyō]] user ever will: not just the presence and rough strength of aura, but its exact structure, flow, and composition, down to the shape of another practitioner's [[Nen Ability]] as it's being formed.

The practical effect is efficiency bordering on the absurd. A Six Eyes bearer wastes almost nothing — they can see precisely how much aura a given effect requires and spend exactly that, sustaining output all day that would leave any other user hollowed out within minutes. Combined with the clan's ordinary talent for Nen, it produces practitioners who are less "very strong" than categorically operating on different terms from everyone around them.

The trait's rarity has shaped the clan more than the trait itself. Gojo without the Six Eyes are simply a wealthy, well-trained Nen family; a Gojo *with* them becomes the single most important political fact of their generation, treated as an asset to be positioned rather than a person, and the clan's internal politics are largely built around who controls that person. Bearers who resent the arrangement have a habit of becoming very difficult for the clan to control at all.
