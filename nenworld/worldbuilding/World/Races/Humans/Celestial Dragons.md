# Celestial Dragons

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]] › [[Humans]]

The Celestial Dragons are the descendants of the kings who founded the world's central authority, and they are not a clan — they're a **caste**. Dozens of entirely separate noble houses hold the status, sharing no common family line beyond the founding generation and frequently loathing one another. The [[Donquixote]] are one such house among many.

Physically they are ordinary humans, and that is precisely the point of them. A Celestial Dragon has no inherited trait, no bloodline gift, and often no combat ability whatsoever. What they have is legal untouchability: by the law of the central authority, harming one is an offence answered with overwhelming force, which means they can take property, land, or people on sight and be obeyed. Whole regions have been ruined over one of them being mildly inconvenienced.

The caste's habits follow from never being told no. Many refuse to breathe the same air as commoners and travel inside sealed helmets fed by their own supply; most have never learned to do anything for themselves; and generations of marrying strictly within the caste to keep the bloodline "pure" have left a great many of them sickly, fragile, or inbred to the point of genuine impairment. Individuals occasionally renounce the status and vanish into the wider world — a scandal each time, and the only way any of them ever becomes a person worth knowing.
