# Marechi

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]] › [[Humans]]

Marechi is rare blood. Roughly one person in several tens of thousands is born with it, and it appears at random in entirely unrelated families — no lineage carries it reliably, no clan can claim it, and two Marechi have no more connection to each other than any two strangers. A Marechi is physically an ordinary human in every other respect and will usually live their whole life without learning what they are.

The problem is what it does to [[Vampires|Vampires]]. Marechi blood is overwhelming to them: the scent alone carries further than ordinary blood and hits far harder, and a single Marechi is reckoned worth dozens of ordinary people to something that feeds on them. A Vampire that catches the scent will abandon a hunt, a plan, or a fight to chase it, and even Originals — who are otherwise almost impossible to bait — have been drawn out of hiding by it.

This makes the Marechi both the most endangered people in any Vampire-troubled region and the most useful. Communities that know what they have tend to hide them; hunters who know what they have tend to *use* them, since a Marechi is the closest thing that exists to reliable bait for something that normally chooses when and where it appears. Being born Marechi is not a gift or a power. It's a target painted on someone who did nothing to earn it, and the sharper question is always who finds out first.
