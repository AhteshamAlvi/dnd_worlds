## Humans

Humans are the baseline this whole world is measured against — the most numerous people by far, found in every kingdom, culture, and corner of it, and the species every other race here gets compared to when someone says "stronger than a human" or "passes for human." On their own, humans have no inherited racial gift: no claws, no elemental affinity, no built-in edge over anyone else. What they have instead is sheer range — the same species produces farmers, scholars, kings, and history's strongest Nen users, purely through will, training, and circumstance rather than anything handed to them at birth.

That blank slate is the point. Because humans start with nothing extra, whatever they become is built rather than born, and it's exactly why [[Nen]] took root the way it did — a system anyone can learn, with no race gatekeeping who gets to be powerful. The result is that humans hold their own against races built from monsters, gods, and magic bloodlines not despite having no innate trait, but because they never needed one.

Not every human stays purely human, of course — bloodlines change, chance intervenes, and the rest of this page covers how.

## Human Subtypes

Human subtypes are still human — same species, capable of everything an ordinary human is, and like anyone else can be a [[Nen]] user — but they carry a distinct lineage or bloodline that leaves them **physically different from the baseline** in some inherited, bodily way. They are usually either a descendent of a [[Bloodkin]], or the descendants of a hybrid between a human and a member of another race. They can also be descendants of [[Constructs]] (artificially constructed or modified beings). A few of the groups below are exceptions to the "physically different" rule — set apart by caste, circumstance, or something nobody has satisfactorily explained rather than by the body — but they're collected here because they're the same kind of thing: a category of human that isn't the baseline.

Subtypes come in two shapes. A **[[Clans|Clan]]** is contained — one traceable family, everyone in it descended from the same ancestors. The rest are **scattered**: the same kind of trait or condition turning up across many unrelated people and families, with no shared bloodline holding them together.

**Scattered (not family lines):**
- [[Bloodkin]] — humans remade with the blood or essence of a powerful creature
- [[Quincy]]
- [[Pyrokinetic]]
- [[Fullbringer]] — mothers survived a [[Hollow]] attack while pregnant; unrelated to one another entirely
- [[Jinchūriki]] — a [[Monsters|Monster]] sealed into a human vessel, made individually and never inherited
- [[Marechi]] — rare blood that drives [[Vampires]] into a frenzy; appears at random in unrelated families
- [[Celestial Dragons]] — a hereditary *caste* of many separate noble houses, not one family

**Contained (family lines):**
- [[Clans]] — [[Uchiha]], [[Hyuga]], [[Kaguya]], [[Fanalis]], etc.

## Mutations

Not every human oddity is inherited. A **[[Mutations|Mutation]]** is a one-off, unrepeatable deviation from the human baseline. Where a Human Subtype is a trait a whole lineage carries and a [[Constructs|Construct]] is built the same way on purpose, a Mutation is neither inherited nor designed: someone born with extra limbs to entirely ordinary parents, someone changed by a rare mineral or a strange lake, or even someone born permanently unable to use Nen at all. See [[Mutations]] for the full breakdown.
