# Constructs

[[Ideal Power System For the Real World]] › [[World Building]] › [[Race]]

Constructs are beings that were made rather than born. Where every other race reproduces or arises on its own, a Construct is the deliberate product of someone's craft. They are built, grown, assembled, or animated to serve a purpose, and only sometimes outliving or outgrowing that purpose to become a person in its own right.

There is no single way to make one. Constructs have been:

- **grown or engineered from flesh** — bodies stitched from corpses or cultured from tissue and forced into a living, often monstrous shape
- **mass-produced as clones** — grown by the hundreds from a single template to serve as a disposable army
- **animated as puppets** — inert bodies or crafted shells driven by an operator's will or a stored power
- **built as machines or cyborgs** — mechanical or half-mechanical bodies given autonomy
- **housed as artificial minds** — a manufactured consciousness poured into a vessel or a borrowed body
- **replicated as doubles** — copies grown or conjured to mirror an existing person
- **made by accident** — the unintended byproduct of an experiment gone wrong, alive and self-sustaining despite no one meaning to create them, sometimes forced to feed on others to keep themselves going

The result runs the full range from mindless tool to fully sapient individual. Some Constructs never have a will of their own; others wake up, question what they are, and walk away from whoever built them. A Construct can be as powerful as its maker was skilled, and like anyone else, one with a mind of its own can learn [[Nen]].
